# Choose where your job is distributed

> Decide which channels broadcast your role and reach the right audience.

## Before you start
- Make sure your career hub is set up if you want to use it as the primary destination.
- Premium channels may use credits from your wallet — check your balance.

## Steps

1. While creating or editing a job, open the **Distribution** step.
   ![Distribution step](./assets/01-distribution-step.png)
2. Toggle on the free channels you want — your career hub is included by default.
   ![Free channels toggles](./assets/02-free-channels.png)
3. Switch to the **Premium** tab to add paid boards. The cost in credits is shown next to each option.
   ![Premium channels](./assets/03-premium-channels.png)
4. Continue to the final step. When you publish, your job is pushed to every selected channel.

## Tips
- Start with your career hub plus one or two relevant boards — you can always add more later.
- Track which channels deliver the best applicants from the **Insights** tab on the job.

## Troubleshooting
- **A premium channel didn't post** — make sure your wallet has enough credits, then republish.
- **Career hub is missing from the list** — set up your career hub from the **Career hub** module first.
