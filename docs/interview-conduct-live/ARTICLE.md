# Conduct a live interview

> Join the interview room from Tech1m so you have the candidate, plan, and notepad together.

## Before you start
- Run the **Speed test** from the room lobby on first use to confirm your camera, mic, and bandwidth are fine.
- Allow camera, microphone, and screen-share permissions in your browser.

## Steps

1. From the Interview Suite, click **Join** on the live interview row, or open the calendar invite link.
   ![Lobby and speed test](./assets/01-lobby.png)
2. The interview room opens with the candidate brief on the left, the interview plan in the middle, and your scratchpad on the right.
3. Walk through plan sections in order. Tick each section as you finish so other panelists know where you are.
   ![Live interview panels](./assets/02-live-panels.png)
4. Use the notepad to capture quotes and observations. Notes save automatically and feed into your scorecard later.
5. When you are done, click **End interview**. The recording, transcript, and AI insights begin processing right away.

## Tips
- Use **Tag a moment** to mark a key answer; you can jump straight to that timestamp in the recording later.
- For panel interviews, only one evaluator needs to start the recording - everyone else's notes are still saved.

## Troubleshooting
- **Audio drops out** — leave and rejoin. Tech1m keeps the recording running as long as one participant remains.
- **The candidate cannot get in** — confirm they are using the latest invite link; old links from rescheduled interviews stop working.
