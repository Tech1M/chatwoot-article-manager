# Request time off

> Submit a time off request so your manager can approve it, your balance is updated, and timesheets reflect the days you are away.

## Before you start
- You know the dates you need off and which leave type applies.
- Your time off balance has enough days for the request.
- You know who your approver is, in case you want to give them a heads up.

## Steps

1. Open **Operations → Time off** and click **Request time off**.
   ![Time off page with the request button](./assets/01-time-off-page.png)
2. Pick the leave type (vacation, sick, personal, or whichever your company offers), choose the start and end dates, and optionally add a half-day for the first or last day.
   ![Time off request form with type and dates](./assets/02-request-form.png)
3. Add a short reason if your company requires one, then submit. Your request now shows as **Pending** in your list and your approver is notified.
   ![Pending request in the time off list](./assets/03-pending-request.png)
4. You will get a notification when the request is approved or declined. Approved days are deducted from your balance and skipped on your timesheet.

## Tips
- Submit requests as early as possible — some leave types have a minimum notice window.
- Cancel a pending request from its detail page if your plans change.

## Troubleshooting
- **Not enough balance** — the request will be blocked. Talk to your manager or HR about an exception or unpaid leave.
- **Date overlaps an existing request** — edit one of the requests to remove the overlap before submitting.
