# Make and send an offer

> Build the offer, send it to the candidate, and watch their response without leaving the application.

## Before you start
- Have the offer details ready: start date, compensation, role, manager, and any custom terms.
- Confirm whoever signs offers on your side has access — their name appears on the offer letter.

## Steps

1. Open the candidate's application and click **Make offer**. The offer builder opens with the role and candidate already filled in.
   ![Make offer](./assets/01-make-offer.png)
2. Fill in compensation, start date, and any extras (bonus, equity, benefits). Pick an offer letter template if your team has one — the variables are filled in automatically.
   ![Offer details](./assets/02-offer-details.png)
3. Review the generated letter. Edit any wording inline before sending.
4. Click **Send offer**. The candidate gets an email with a secure link to view, accept, or decline. The application moves to the **Offered** stage.
   ![Send offer](./assets/03-send-offer.png)
5. Track status from the application: **Sent**, **Viewed**, **Accepted**, or **Declined**. Use **Send reminder** if the candidate hasn't responded after a few days, or **Cancel offer** to withdraw it.

## Tips
- If you need to change a number after sending, cancel the offer and send a new one — candidates can't sign two competing offers.
- Once accepted, click **Hire** on the application to close the loop and lock the record.

## Troubleshooting
- **The candidate says they didn't get the email** — check their activity log for the send event, then click **Resend**. Ask them to check spam.
- **You can't edit a sent offer** — sent offers are read-only. Cancel and resend with the corrected details.
