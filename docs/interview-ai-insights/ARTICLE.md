# Read AI interview insights

> Tech1m turns each recording into a short summary, themed highlights, and signal scores you can use as a starting point for your scorecard.

## Before you start
- Insights are generated automatically once the recording and transcript finish processing.
- A scorecard template attached to the interview helps Tech1m align the insights to the competencies you actually rate.

## Steps

1. Open a completed interview and switch to the **AI insights** tab.
   ![AI insights overview](./assets/01-insights.png)
2. Read the **Summary** for a 5 to 10 sentence recap of the conversation.
3. Review the **Signal scores** - one per competency in the scorecard, each with the evidence that supports it linked back to transcript timestamps.
   ![Signal scores with evidence](./assets/02-signals.png)
4. Check **Follow-up suggestions** for questions worth asking in the next round if the candidate moves forward.
5. Click any evidence chip to jump to the exact moment in the recording.

## Tips
- AI insights are a draft, not a verdict. Always reconcile them with your own scorecard.
- Use the **Copy summary** button to paste the recap into a hiring decision discussion.

## Troubleshooting
- **Insights are blank** — the interview may have been too short, mostly silent, or in a language Tech1m does not yet support.
- **Signal scores feel off** — make sure the scorecard template attached to the interview reflects the competencies you actually want to measure.
