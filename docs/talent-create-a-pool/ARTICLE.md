# Create a talent pool

> Learn how to set up a talent pool and start collecting candidates for current and future roles.

## Before you start
- You need permission to view and manage the Talent CRM.
- Decide what the pool represents — a role family, a region, an event, or a hiring round.

## Steps

1. From the sidebar, open **Talent CRM**. You will land on your list of pools.
   ![Talent CRM home](./assets/01-talent-crm-home.png)
2. Click **New pool** in the top-right.
   ![New pool button](./assets/02-new-pool-button.png)
3. Give the pool a clear name and short description so teammates know its purpose. You can also pick a color or icon to make it easy to spot.
   ![Pool details form](./assets/03-pool-details.png)
4. Click **Create pool**. You will be taken straight into the empty pool, ready for you to add candidates.
   ![Empty pool view](./assets/04-empty-pool.png)

## Tips
- Use focused pools (for example, "Senior frontend — Lagos") rather than one giant catch-all. Search and bulk actions work better on smaller, well-defined groups.
- You can rename or delete a pool later from its settings menu.
- Pools are private to your company. Only teammates with CRM access can see them.

## Troubleshooting
- **I do not see the New pool button** — your role may not include CRM management permissions. Ask an admin to grant access.
- **The pool is missing from my list** — clear any active filter or search at the top of the Talent CRM page.
