# Invite team members

> Bring your hiring team into the workspace so they can post jobs, review candidates, and run interviews with you.

## Before you start
- The work email of each person you want to invite.
- A rough idea of what each person should be able to do (see [Roles and permissions explained](../getting-started-roles-and-permissions/ARTICLE.md)).

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Team**.
   ![Team settings](./assets/01-team-settings.png)
2. Click **Add team member**, then enter the person's name, work email, and role. Optionally pick their department and job title.
   ![Add team member dialog](./assets/02-add-member.png)
3. Click **Send invitation**. They get an email with a link to set a password and join your workspace.
   ![Pending invitation in list](./assets/03-pending-invite.png)
4. To resend an invitation that hasn't been accepted yet, open the row and choose **Resend invitation**. To remove someone, choose **Remove from workspace**.
   ![Resend or remove member](./assets/04-resend-remove.png)

## Tips
- You can change someone's role or department later from the same screen.
- New members start with no jobs assigned. Add them as collaborators on a job to give them access to its candidates.

## Troubleshooting
- **Invitation expired** — open the member's row and click **Resend invitation** to send a fresh link.
- **Person says they didn't get the email** — ask them to check spam, then resend. Make sure you used their correct work email.
