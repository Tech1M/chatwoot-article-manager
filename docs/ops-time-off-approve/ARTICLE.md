# Approve time off

> Review pending requests from people you manage, check who else is out, and approve or decline.

## Before you start
- You are listed as the approver for the requester's team.
- You know your team's coverage for the requested period.
- You understand your company's leave policy for the relevant leave type.

## Steps

1. Open **Operations → Time off → Pending** to see every request waiting on you.
   ![Pending requests queue](./assets/01-pending-queue.png)
2. Click a request to see the dates, leave type, current balance, and the team calendar showing other people already off in that range.
   ![Time off request detail with team calendar](./assets/02-request-detail.png)
3. Choose **Approve** to confirm, or **Decline** with a reason. The requester is notified immediately and their balance and timesheet update on approval.
   ![Approve and decline actions on a request](./assets/03-approve-decline.png)
4. If you need a change rather than a flat decline, ask the requester via the request comments to update and resubmit.

## Tips
- Use the team calendar to spot conflicts before approving back-to-back leave.
- Decline reasons are visible to the requester — keep them respectful and clear.

## Troubleshooting
- **The request does not show in my queue** — confirm you are set as the approver in that team's settings.
- **Approval did not update payroll** — payroll runs on a schedule; the change appears on the next cycle.
