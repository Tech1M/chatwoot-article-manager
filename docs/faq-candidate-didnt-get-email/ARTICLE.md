# A candidate didn't receive my email

> A candidate says they never got your message, or you don't see a reply you expected.

## Symptom

You sent an email — an invite, an offer, a follow-up — and the candidate hasn't responded. You're not sure if it left your workspace or got lost on their side.

## Most likely cause: the email is in their spam folder or was bounced

Most "missing" emails are filtered, not lost. A small number bounce because the address is wrong or the inbox is full.

1. Open the candidate's profile and go to the **Activity** tab.
2. Find the email you sent and check its delivery status.
   ![Email delivery status on candidate profile](./assets/01-delivery-status.png)
3. If it shows **Delivered**, ask the candidate to check Spam, Promotions, and Updates folders, then add your sender address to their contacts.
4. If it shows **Bounced** or **Failed**, confirm the email address is spelled correctly and update it on the profile.
5. Click **Resend** to retry, or use **Reply** to send a fresh copy from the conversation thread.

## Other things to check

- The candidate's inbox is full — ask them to clear space and try again.
- A typo in the address (common: `.con` instead of `.com`).
- The candidate previously unsubscribed or marked one of your emails as spam, blocking future sends.
- Your workspace admin disabled outgoing email for your role.
- The candidate's company blocks external senders. Ask for a personal address.

## When to contact support

Contact us if delivery shows **Delivered** but the candidate has searched all folders and still can't find it, or if every email to a specific domain is bouncing. Share the candidate's email address and the time you sent the message.
