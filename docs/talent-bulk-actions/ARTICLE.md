# Run bulk actions on candidates

> Get repetitive work done in seconds by acting on a whole list of candidates at once.

## Before you start
- Open a talent pool and use search or filters to narrow down to the candidates you want to act on.
- Make sure you have permission to manage the pool you are working in.

## Steps

1. Tick the checkboxes next to each candidate, or use the **Select all** box at the top of the list to grab the entire filtered set.
   ![Select candidates](./assets/01-select-candidates.png)
2. The bulk action bar appears at the bottom of the screen, showing the count of selected candidates.
   ![Bulk action bar](./assets/02-bulk-action-bar.png)
3. Choose an action:
   - **Tag** — add a shared tag to everyone selected.
   - **Move to pool** — copy or move them to another pool.
   - **Favorite** — mark all as favorite.
   - **Archive** — hide from the active list without deleting.
   - **Remove from pool** — take them out of this pool only.
4. Confirm the action. A progress toast shows when it is complete.
   ![Action confirmation](./assets/03-action-confirmation.png)

## Tips
- Apply a search filter first, then click **Select all** — you will only act on the filtered subset, not the whole pool.
- Bulk actions are logged in each candidate's activity, so your team can see what happened and when.

## Troubleshooting
- **Action button is greyed out** — your role may not include the permission for that action.
- **Some candidates were skipped** — they may already have the tag, or already exist in the destination pool. The summary lists what changed.
