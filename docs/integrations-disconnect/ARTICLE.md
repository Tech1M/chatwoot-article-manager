# Disconnect an integration

> Disconnect a provider when you've switched tools, want to reconnect with a different account, or no longer want Tech1m sharing data with it.

## Before you start
- You can only disconnect integrations that you connected. A teammate's connection stays put until they remove it themselves.
- Disconnecting stops future syncs immediately. Data already imported into Tech1m (candidates, events, messages) stays where it is.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Integrations**. Connected tools appear at the top of the page.
   ![Connected integrations list](./assets/01-connected-list.png)
2. Click the menu on the integration's tile and choose **Disconnect**.
   ![Disconnect option in tile menu](./assets/02-disconnect-action.png)
3. Confirm. The tile moves back to the marketplace below, ready to reconnect whenever you want.
   ![Confirmation that the integration is disconnected](./assets/03-disconnected.png)

## Tips
- To switch the account behind a connection (for example, moving from a personal to a shared ATS login), disconnect first and then connect again with the new account.
- If a colleague leaves your team, ask them to disconnect their integrations before their account is closed so nothing keeps trying to sync.

## Troubleshooting
- **You can't see a Disconnect option** — only the teammate who connected this tool can remove it. Ask them, or have an admin handle it.
- **The integration still appears as connected after disconnecting** — refresh the page. The state catches up after a moment.
- **You disconnected by mistake** — just click **Connect** again and reauthorize. Your previous imports stay intact.
