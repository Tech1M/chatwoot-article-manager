# Manage shifts and rosters

> Define your shift patterns once, then assign and publish a roster that everyone can see.

## Before you start
- A list of the workers you want to schedule (contractors, staff, or both).
- The shift patterns you operate — for example **Day**, **Night**, **Weekend**, or named site shifts.

## Steps

1. Open **Roster** from the sidebar. The grid shows people on the left and the dates across the top.
   ![Roster grid](./assets/01-roster-grid.png)
2. Click **Configure shifts** to add or edit shift types. Set the label, colour, default start and end time, and whether breaks are paid.
   ![Configure shift types](./assets/02-configure-shifts.png)
3. Back on the grid, click an empty cell and pick a shift type to assign it. Drag across cells to assign the same shift to several days at once.
4. Use **Assign shift** for bulk changes — pick a group of workers, the date range, and the shift pattern, then confirm.
5. Click **Publish** to release the roster. Everyone you assigned receives a notification with their schedule and can see it in their workspace.

## Tips
- Switch between **Week** and **Month** views in the roster header for short-term and long-term planning.
- Public holidays and approved leave are pre-shaded on the grid so you do not double-book.

## Troubleshooting
- **A worker is missing from the grid** — they are inactive or their engagement has not started yet. Check the start date on their profile.
- **Cannot assign a shift on a specific day** — the worker has approved leave or a public holiday. Cancel the leave or override from the cell menu.
