# Use AI suggestions

> Turn a generic step template into a message that sounds like you wrote it for this candidate.

## Before you start
- The candidate needs to be enrolled in a sequence with at least one step.
- Your sequence step should have a template body — AI suggestions personalize *your* message, they don't write from scratch.

## Steps

1. Open a task from the **Tasks** queue, or a candidate's enrollment from the sequence detail page.
   ![Task with AI suggest button](./assets/01-task.png)
2. Click **AI suggest**. Tech1m drafts a message using the step template, your prompt hint, and the candidate's role, headline, and company.
   ![AI suggested message](./assets/02-suggested-message.png)
3. Review and edit the draft until it sounds right, then send it through the relevant channel and mark the task done.

## Tips
- Add a clear **AI prompt hint** when you build the sequence step — *"Mention their open-source work if any"* — to steer suggestions.
- Keep the template short and natural; AI suggestions amplify the tone you set.
- Always read the draft before sending. AI gets candidate context right most of the time, but a quick review catches the rest.

## Troubleshooting
- **Suggestion unavailable** — the AI service is briefly offline. Try again in a moment, or send the template as-is.
- **Suggestions feel generic** — strengthen the prompt hint and make sure the candidate has a headline and current company on their profile.
