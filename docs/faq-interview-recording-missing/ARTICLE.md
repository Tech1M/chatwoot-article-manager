# An interview recording is missing

> You finished an interview but the recording or transcript hasn't appeared on the interview page.

## Symptom

You return to the interview after the call and the **Recording** or **Transcript** tab is empty, shows "Processing", or says no recording was captured.

## Most likely cause: the recording is still processing

Recordings are uploaded and transcribed after the call ends. Long sessions or large videos can take several minutes — sometimes longer during busy periods.

1. Open the interview from your calendar or the **Interviews** tab.
2. Look at the status banner at the top of the page.
   ![Recording processing banner](./assets/01-processing-status.png)
3. If it says **Processing**, wait 10–15 minutes for a one-hour interview, then refresh.
4. If it says **No recording captured**, the call ended before recording started or recording was turned off — see the checks below.
5. Once ready, the **Transcript** and **AI insights** tabs unlock automatically.

## Other things to check

- Recording was disabled for that interview. Open **Edit interview** and confirm **Record this session** is on.
- A participant declined the consent prompt at the start of the call. Recording stops if any required participant declines.
- The host left before any other participant joined — interviews need a host present to record.
- The interview was held outside our meeting room (e.g., a personal Zoom link). Only interviews started from our scheduler are recorded.
- The candidate disconnected very early — short sessions under one minute aren't kept.

## When to contact support

Get in touch if more than an hour has passed, the status still shows **Processing**, and the interview was at least a few minutes long. Send the interview link and the date and time it took place.
