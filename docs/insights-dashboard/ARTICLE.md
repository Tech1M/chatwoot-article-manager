# Insights dashboard overview

> Get a single-screen view of how your hiring is performing across jobs, candidates, and the team.

## Before you start
- Have at least one active job with applications so charts are populated.
- Make sure your role has the **View insights** permission.

## Steps

1. From the left sidebar, click **Insights** to open the dashboard.
   ![Insights menu in sidebar](./assets/01-open-insights.png)
2. Use the date range picker at the top right to pick a window — last 7 days, last 30 days, this quarter, or a custom range. All charts update together.
   ![Date range picker](./assets/02-date-range.png)
3. Scan the summary cards for headline numbers: open jobs, new applicants, hires, and average time-to-fill. Click any card to drill into the underlying list.
4. Scroll to the **Activity** and **Source** charts to see where applicants are coming from and which recruiters are most active.
   ![Activity and source charts](./assets/03-activity.png)
5. Use the job filter to narrow every chart to a single job, department, or location.

## Tips
- Pin the dashboard to your home screen from your profile preferences for one-click access.
- Hover any chart segment to see exact counts and percentages.

## Troubleshooting
- **Numbers look low** — check the date range; it defaults to the last 30 days.
- **A chart is empty** — there is no data in the selected window. Widen the range or remove filters.
