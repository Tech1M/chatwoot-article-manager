# Handle timesheet disputes

> Open a dispute when a contractor's logged hours do not match what was actually worked, and resolve it together before payroll closes.

## Before you start
- The timesheet in question has been submitted.
- You can point to specific days or entries that are incorrect.
- You have evidence such as Slack threads, calendar invites, or work output.

## Steps

1. Open the timesheet and choose **Raise issue** on the entry you disagree with. Add a reason and any evidence.
   ![Raise issue action on a timesheet entry](./assets/01-raise-issue.png)
2. The contractor is notified and replies with their explanation. Both sides can attach files and exchange messages inside the dispute thread.
   ![Timesheet dispute conversation](./assets/02-dispute-thread.png)
3. Agree on a resolution: adjust hours, accept as logged, or remove the entry. Click **Resolve** and pick the outcome — the timesheet updates accordingly.
   ![Resolve dispute with outcome options](./assets/03-resolve-outcome.png)
4. Once resolved, approve the corrected timesheet so payroll can include it in the current cycle.

## Tips
- Open issues at the entry level rather than disputing the whole timesheet.
- Resolve disputes before the payroll cutoff to avoid manual adjustments.

## Troubleshooting
- **Cannot raise an issue** — the timesheet must be submitted, not still in draft.
- **Resolution did not update payroll** — payroll may have already locked; contact your finance lead to apply an adjustment.
