# Access recordings and transcripts

> Replay any interview, jump to specific moments, and search the transcript for keywords.

## Before you start
- Recording must have been enabled when the interview ran. Live interviews started inside Tech1m record by default.
- Processing can take a few minutes after the call ends.

## Steps

1. Open the interview from the Interview Suite or from the candidate's application timeline.
2. Switch to the **Recording** tab to play back the video and audio.
   ![Recording playback](./assets/01-recording.png)
3. Switch to the **Transcript** tab to read a speaker-labeled, time-stamped transcript. Click any line to jump to that point in the recording.
   ![Transcript with speakers](./assets/02-transcript.png)
4. Use the search box to find keywords across the whole transcript.
5. Use **Share** to send a private playback link to other staff on the hiring team. Permissions follow the candidate's job permissions.

## Tips
- Download the transcript as a text file for offline review or to attach to an interview report.
- Tagged moments appear as markers on the playback timeline.

## Troubleshooting
- **Recording is missing** — the interview may have been hosted outside Tech1m or recording was disabled in the interview plan.
- **Transcript looks wrong** — accuracy depends on audio quality. Use the speaker map at the top of the transcript to fix mislabeled speakers.
