# Use the supplier directory

> The supplier directory is your single view of every external partner — who they are, what they're working on, and what stage they're at.

## Before you start
- You'll need permission to view suppliers in your company.

## Steps

1. Open **Suppliers** from the sidebar. The directory lists every supplier your company has added, with their name, category, contact details, and current status.
   ![Supplier directory](./assets/01-supplier-directory.png)
2. Filter by status — **Active**, **Under review**, or **Inactive** — to focus on the suppliers you care about right now. Use the search box to jump to a specific name.
3. Click a supplier to open their profile. You'll see their contact, the work assigned to them (such as open requisitions), and the history of how they've performed.
   ![Supplier profile](./assets/02-supplier-profile.png)

## Tips
- Use **Under review** as a working list — suppliers you've invited but who haven't completed onboarding yet.
- Keep the contact details up to date. Reminders and intake requests go to the email on file.

## Troubleshooting
- **A supplier you added isn't showing up** — check the status filter; new invitees sit under **Under review** until they accept.
- **You can't see the directory** — your role may not include the suppliers permission. Ask your admin to update it.
