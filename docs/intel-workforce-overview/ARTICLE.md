# Workforce intelligence overview

> Get a clearer picture of your existing team — who they are, what they do well, and where you have gaps.

## Before you start
- Make sure your team roster is up to date in Tech1m.
- Ask team members to complete their skills profile so coverage charts are meaningful.

## Steps

1. From the sidebar, click **Workforce intelligence** to open the overview.
   ![Workforce intelligence menu](./assets/01-open.png)
2. Use the filters at the top to scope by department, location, or employment type.
3. Review the **Composition** panel for headcount, tenure, and demographic breakdowns.
   ![Composition panel](./assets/02-composition.png)
4. Open the **Skills coverage** map to see which capabilities are well represented and which are thin. Click any skill to see who holds it.
5. Check the **Engagement** strip for the latest survey scores, manager check-in frequency, and flight-risk indicators.
   ![Skills and engagement](./assets/03-skills-engagement.png)

## Tips
- Pair skills gaps with open requisitions to prioritize hiring or upskilling.
- Export any panel as CSV from its menu to share with people partners.

## Troubleshooting
- **Skills look empty** — team members have not completed their profiles yet; send a reminder from the panel.
- **Engagement is missing** — no survey has been run recently. Start one from Workstyle or Engagement.
