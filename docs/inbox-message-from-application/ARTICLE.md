# Message a candidate from an application

> Reaching out from inside an application keeps the conversation linked to the right job, so anyone reviewing the candidate later sees the full history.

## Before you start
- The candidate must have a valid email or phone number on file for the channel you choose.
- Your role needs permission to message candidates on the job.

## Steps

1. Open the application from the **Applications** tab on a job, or from the candidate's profile. Click **Message** in the action bar.
   ![Message action on application](./assets/01-message-action.png)
2. Pick a channel — email or SMS — and write your message. You can insert a template or use Ammie to draft a first pass.
   ![Compose message](./assets/02-compose.png)
3. Send the message. The conversation now appears in your **Inbox** with the application as its context, so replies land in the same thread.
   ![Conversation linked to application](./assets/03-linked.png)

## Tips
- The first message creates the conversation. Reply from the Inbox afterwards — there's no need to start a new thread.
- Keep your first message short and specific; it dramatically improves reply rates.

## Troubleshooting
- **No SMS option** — the candidate hasn't shared a phone number, or your plan doesn't include SMS outreach.
- **Message bounced** — confirm the candidate's email isn't a typo on the application; ask them to update their profile if needed.
