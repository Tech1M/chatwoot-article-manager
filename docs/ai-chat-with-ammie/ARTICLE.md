# Chat with Ammie

> Ammie is the conversational AI assistant built into Tech1m. Ask her to draft, summarise, or sanity-check anything you'd ask a smart teammate.

## Before you start
- Ammie is available to any team member with access to the app — no special role required.
- Each Ammie response uses AI credits. Check your wallet in **Billing** if you run out.

## Steps

1. Open Ammie from the assistant icon in the top bar, or from the **Chat with Ammie** panel inside any job brief.
   ![Open Ammie panel](./assets/01-open-ammie.png)
2. Type your prompt in plain English — for example, "Draft a screening question that filters for senior React engineers" or "Summarise this candidate's experience." Ammie keeps context for the current chat, so you can ask follow-ups without repeating yourself.
   ![Ammie prompt input](./assets/02-prompt.png)
3. Use the action buttons under each reply to copy the response, regenerate it, or apply it directly to the job brief you're editing.
   ![Apply Ammie response](./assets/03-apply.png)

## Tips
- Start a new chat when you switch topics — it gives Ammie a clean slate and produces sharper answers.
- Ammie is best at drafting first passes. Always review her output before sending it to a candidate.

## Troubleshooting
- **Ammie won't open** — refresh the page; if she still doesn't appear, your role may not have AI tools enabled.
- **Replies are cut off** — ask Ammie to "continue" or break your request into smaller steps.
- **Out of credits** — top up from **Billing → Purchase credits**.
