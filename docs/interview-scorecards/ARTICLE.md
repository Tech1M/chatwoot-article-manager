# Score an interview

> Capture your judgment on a candidate with a consistent scorecard so the hiring committee has comparable inputs.

## Before you start
- A scorecard template must be attached to the interview. The template usually comes from the interview plan.
- You should have your interview notes from the live session ready.

## Steps

1. Open the interview and click **Open scorecard**, or use the prompt at the top of the Interview Suite when a scorecard is overdue.
   ![Scorecard prompt](./assets/01-prompt.png)
2. Rate each competency on the scale set by the template. Add a short comment explaining the rating.
3. Use the **Notes** and **AI insights** panels on the side as memory aids. Drag transcript snippets into the comment box as evidence.
   ![Scorecard with evidence](./assets/02-rating.png)
4. Choose your overall recommendation: strong hire, hire, no hire, or strong no hire.
5. Click **Submit scorecard**. Once submitted, the rating is visible to the rest of the hiring team but cannot be edited without approval.

## Tips
- Submit your scorecard before reading other evaluators' ratings to keep the panel independent.
- If you are uncertain, save as a draft and come back. Drafts are private to you.

## Troubleshooting
- **No scorecard attached** — ask a hiring manager or admin to add a template to the interview plan, then refresh.
- **Cannot submit** — every required competency needs a rating. Required fields are marked with an asterisk.
