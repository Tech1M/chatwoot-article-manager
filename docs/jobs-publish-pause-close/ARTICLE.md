# Publish, pause, or close a job

> Keep your hiring pipeline accurate by moving each job through the right state.

## Before you start
- A draft job needs every required step completed before it can be published.
- Closing a job stops new applications but keeps the existing pipeline intact.

## Steps

1. Open the job from the **Jobs** list.
   ![Job actions menu](./assets/01-actions-menu.png)
2. Click **Publish** if it's still a draft. Confirm where the job should be distributed.
   ![Publish modal](./assets/02-publish-modal.png)
3. To temporarily stop new applications, click **Pause**. The job stays visible to your team but is hidden from candidates.
   ![Pause action](./assets/03-pause.png)
4. When hiring is done — or you no longer need the role — click **Close**. You'll be asked to confirm; the job moves to the closed list.
   ![Close action](./assets/04-close.png)

## Tips
- Pause instead of closing if you might restart hiring soon — paused jobs keep all settings.
- Use the **Reopen** option on a closed job to bring it back without re-creating it.

## Troubleshooting
- **Publish is greyed out** — at least one required step still has missing information.
- **Candidates can still see a paused job** — search engines may have cached the page; allow a few hours to clear.
