# Set an interview plan template

> Define your standard interview rounds once, then apply them to every new job.

## Before you start
- Decide on your default interview rounds — for example screening call, technical, panel, final.
- Have a list of evaluators or roles for each round if you want them pre-assigned.

## Steps

1. Open **Jobs settings** and click the **Interview plans** tab.
   ![Interview plans tab](./assets/01-plans-tab.png)
2. Click **New plan**, name it, and add each round in order — round name, duration, and interview format.
   ![New plan form](./assets/02-new-plan.png)
3. For each round, pick the scorecard and any default questions you want interviewers to use.
   ![Round configuration](./assets/03-round-config.png)
4. Save the plan. When you create a new job, apply this plan from the **Interview plan** picker.
   ![Apply plan to job](./assets/04-apply.png)

## Tips
- Keep one default plan and a couple of variations (e.g. "Engineering" and "Sales") rather than one per role.
- Update the plan when your process changes — existing jobs keep their copy, but new ones use the latest.

## Troubleshooting
- **Plan isn't appearing on new jobs** — confirm it's saved and active. Drafts don't show up.
- **Scorecards aren't loading** — make sure the scorecards exist in your interview settings.
