# Onboard a contractor

> Take a new contractor from invitation to "ready to work" with the right engagement model, compliance checks, and signed contract.

## Before you start
- The contractor's email address and the role they will fill.
- A point of contact on your side (line manager or hiring manager).
- Decide the engagement model: PAYE, umbrella, limited company (PSC), or sole trader.

## Steps

1. Open **Contractors** from the sidebar and click **Onboard contractor**. Pick the role and chain (direct, agency, or umbrella).
   ![Start contractor onboarding](./assets/01-onboard-start.png)
2. Fill in **Role details**, **Location**, and **Engagement model**. Tech1m uses these to decide which compliance steps appear next.
3. Complete the **Worker status** flow. For UK roles this runs IR35, CIS, AWR, and SDS questions where they apply, then records the determination.
   ![Worker status determination](./assets/02-worker-status.png)
4. Run the **Right-to-work check** and any required **Background checks**. The contractor receives a secure link to upload documents.
5. Review the **Contract structure**, pay rates, milestones, and add-ons. Add a Statement of Work if the engagement needs one.
   ![Review contract structure](./assets/03-contract-structure.png)
6. Set the **Approval configuration** — who signs off timesheets, expenses, and milestones — then send the package for the contractor and approvers to review.

## Tips
- Use **Save and continue later** at any step; the onboarding resumes from where you left off.
- The intelligence panel on the right flags missing data and risk before you submit.

## Troubleshooting
- **Worker status step is blocked** — a previous answer is inconsistent. Open the IR35 or SDS questionnaire and re-confirm the disputed item.
- **Contractor cannot upload documents** — the secure link expired. Resend it from the contractor's profile.
