# Manage notification preferences

> Decide when and how Tech1m notifies you about activity across your workspace.

## Before you start
- You must be signed in.
- Notification settings are per user - each teammate manages their own.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings -> Notifications** from the sidebar.
   ![Notifications settings](./assets/01-notifications-entry.png)
2. Toggle each event - new applications, interview reminders, mentions, billing alerts, and so on - **On** or **Off**. The **All notifications** master switch at the top mutes everything in one click.
   ![Notification toggles](./assets/02-notification-toggles.png)
3. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

Your preferences take effect immediately. Existing scheduled emails already in flight may still arrive once.

## Tips
- Turn off categories you do not own (for example, billing if you are not a finance admin) to keep your inbox focused.
- Keep interview and inbox notifications on if you talk to candidates - silent threads are easy to miss.

## Troubleshooting
- **Save button stays disabled** - you have not changed anything yet. Toggle a switch first.
- **Still not getting emails** - check your spam folder, then confirm your work email is correct on the **Profile** tab.
