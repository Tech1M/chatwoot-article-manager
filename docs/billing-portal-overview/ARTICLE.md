# Billing portal overview

> Get a quick lay of the land for everything billing-related in Tech1m.

## Before you start
- You need a workspace admin or billing role to open the billing portal.
- Have a payment method ready if you plan to make changes during the tour.

## Steps

1. From the sidebar, open **Settings** and choose **Billing**. The portal opens to your current plan and a snapshot of your credit balance.
   ![Billing portal landing page](./assets/01-portal-landing.png)
2. Use the side tabs to move between sections:
   - **Plan & credits** — your current plan, credit balance, and one-click top-ups.
   - **Distribution** — upgrade or extend job distribution channels.
   - **Invoices** — every invoice and receipt, ready to download.
   - **Wallet & transactions** — every charge, top-up, refund, and credit movement.
   ![Billing tabs](./assets/02-billing-tabs.png)
3. The header always shows your wallet balance and credit balance, so you can keep an eye on both as you move around.

## Tips
- Pin the billing portal in your sidebar if you check it often.
- Anyone with the billing role gets receipts emailed automatically — invite a teammate from finance to share the load.

## Troubleshooting
- **I don't see Billing in the sidebar** — your role doesn't include billing access. Ask a workspace admin to update your permissions.
- **Numbers look out of date** — refresh the page; the portal pulls live data on each visit.
