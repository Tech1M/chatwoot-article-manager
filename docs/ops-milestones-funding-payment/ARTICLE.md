# Fund and pay milestones

> Reserve funds against a milestone up front so contractors know they will be paid, then release the payment when the work is approved.

## Before you start
- Your wallet has enough balance, or your billing method is connected.
- The milestone has been agreed and added to the signed contract.
- You have permission to release payments on behalf of the company.

## Steps

1. Open the milestone from the contract page and select **Fund milestone**. The amount, currency, and any platform fees are shown before you confirm.
   ![Fund milestone dialog showing amount and fees](./assets/01-fund-dialog.png)
2. Confirm the funding source. Once funded, the milestone status changes to **Funded** and the contractor can see the reserved amount.
   ![Funded badge on the milestone card](./assets/02-funded-badge.png)
3. After you approve the contractor's submission, the **Release payment** action becomes available on the milestone.
   ![Release payment action on an approved milestone](./assets/03-release-payment.png)
4. Click **Release payment** to settle the milestone. The funds move from your reserved balance to the contractor and a payment record is created.

## Tips
- Fund milestones early in the cycle so contractors are not blocked while waiting on review.
- Check the receipt under the milestone for the exact fee breakdown.

## Troubleshooting
- **Funding fails with insufficient balance** — top up your wallet or update your billing method, then try again.
- **Release payment is greyed out** — make sure the milestone submission is approved, not just under review.
