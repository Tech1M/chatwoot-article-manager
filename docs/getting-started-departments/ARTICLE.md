# Create and organize departments

> Departments let you slot jobs and teammates into the right part of your org — Engineering, Sales, People, and so on. They power filtering, reporting, and the right routing for approvals.

## Before you start
- A short list of departments you actually use (don't create one for every project).
- An owner or hiring lead in mind for each.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Departments**.
   ![Departments settings](./assets/01-departments-list.png)
2. Click **New department**. Give it a name, an optional description, and pick a department head.
   ![New department dialog](./assets/02-new-department.png)
3. Save it. To assign teammates, open **Settings → Team**, edit a member, and pick their department.
   ![Assigning a member to a department](./assets/03-assign-member.png)
4. When you create a job, choose its department from the dropdown. You can change it later without losing candidates.

## Tips
- Start with 5–8 departments. You can always split or merge later.
- Use departments for *where someone reports*. Use job collaborators for *who works on a hire*. They're not the same thing.

## Troubleshooting
- **Department won't delete** — make sure no jobs or teammates are still attached. Move them first, then delete.
- **Reports don't show a department** — that means jobs in that department weren't tagged. Open the job and set its department.
