# Create a job

> Set up a new role in a few minutes so candidates can start applying.

## Before you start
- Make sure your company profile and departments are configured.
- Have the role title, location, and basic compensation details handy.

## Steps

1. From the sidebar, open **Jobs** and click **Create job**.
   ![Create job button](./assets/01-create-button.png)
2. Enter the job title, department, employment type, location, and work mode. Add the salary range and currency if you want to share them.
   ![Job basics form](./assets/02-job-basics.png)
3. Write or paste your job description, then add responsibilities, requirements, and skills. You can switch to the AI generator if you want a starting draft.
   ![Job description editor](./assets/03-description.png)
4. Move through the remaining steps — screener questions, blind recruiting, distribution — adjusting each one as needed. You can save and come back later.
5. On the **Review** step, double-check everything and click **Publish** to make the job live, or **Save as draft** to keep working on it.
   ![Review and publish](./assets/04-review.png)

## Tips
- Use a clear, specific title — "Senior Backend Engineer" performs better than "Engineer III".
- Add 5–10 must-have skills so match scoring stays meaningful.

## Troubleshooting
- **Publish button is disabled** — a required field is missing. Scroll back through the steps; the step with the issue shows a red indicator.
- **My job isn't appearing on the career hub** — confirm it's published and that your career hub is enabled.
