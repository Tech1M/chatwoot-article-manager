# Resolve milestone disputes

> When you and a contractor cannot agree on whether a milestone has been delivered, open a dispute so the conversation, evidence, and outcome are tracked in one place.

## Before you start
- You have already tried to resolve the issue through revisions.
- You can describe what was promised versus what was delivered.
- You have any supporting files (chat logs, drafts, references) ready to upload.

## Steps

1. Open the milestone and choose **Raise dispute**. Pick a reason, write a short description of the issue, and attach evidence.
   ![Raise dispute form on a milestone](./assets/01-raise-dispute.png)
2. The contractor is notified and can reply with their own statement and evidence. Each reply is added to the dispute thread.
   ![Dispute conversation thread with replies and attachments](./assets/02-dispute-thread.png)
3. Work toward a resolution: agree on revisions, partial payment, or cancellation. When both sides agree, mark the dispute as **Resolved** and select the outcome.
   ![Resolve dispute action with outcome options](./assets/03-resolve-outcome.png)
4. If you cannot reach agreement, escalate the dispute to Tech1m support from the same panel. An admin will review the evidence and decide.

## Tips
- Keep replies factual and reference specific deliverables or messages.
- Resolve disputes before the next milestone starts so funding flow is not blocked.

## Troubleshooting
- **Cannot raise a dispute** — disputes can only be opened on funded milestones with a submission.
- **Escalation button missing** — the dispute must have at least one reply from each side before it can be escalated.
