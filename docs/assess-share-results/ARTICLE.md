# Share assessment results

> You can share assessment results with the candidate, an internal teammate, or an outside collaborator — each with the right level of detail.

## Before you start
- A reviewed assessment session with a final decision.
- The email address of whoever you're sharing with.

## Steps

1. Open the candidate's session and click **Share**. Pick the recipient type: **Candidate**, **Internal teammate**, or **External collaborator**.
   ![Share dialog](./assets/01-share-dialog.png)
2. Choose what's included — overall score, section breakdown, written answers, reviewer notes. Internal shares can include everything; candidate shares hide the answer key by default.
   ![Share content options](./assets/02-share-content.png)
3. Add a short message and click **Send**. The recipient gets a secure link that expires after the window you set.
   ![Recipient and expiry](./assets/03-recipient-expiry.png)
4. Track who has opened the share link from the **Activity** tab on the session.

## Tips
- For external collaborators, default to time-limited links rather than no expiry.
- Save a frequently-used share message as a template so your team is consistent.

## Troubleshooting
- **Recipient says the link expired** — open the share, click **Renew**, and resend. The original link will start working again.
- **Toggle is greyed out for "include answers"** — your role doesn't allow sharing the key. Ask an admin or a hiring manager.
