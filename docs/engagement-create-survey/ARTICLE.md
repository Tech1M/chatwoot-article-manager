# Create a survey

> Spin up a new survey, add questions, choose your audience, and send it out from the Engagement Centre.

## Before you start
- Make sure you have permission to create surveys in your workspace.
- Decide on the audience (candidates, employees, contractors, or a custom group).
- Have your questions drafted, or pick a template to start from.

## Steps

1. Open **Engagement Centre** from the sidebar and click **New survey**.
   ![Engagement Centre overview](./assets/01-engagement-home.png)
2. Pick a starting point — a blank survey or one of the built-in templates (onboarding, eNPS, exit, pulse).
   ![Survey templates picker](./assets/02-templates.png)
3. Give the survey a title and short description so recipients know what it’s for.
4. Add your questions. You can mix question types like multiple choice, rating, scale, and open text. Drag to reorder.
5. Choose your audience — pick a saved group, a department, or upload a list. Optionally add a personal intro message.
6. Set a delivery window and click **Review & send**. Use **Save as draft** if you want to come back to it later.
   ![Review and send screen](./assets/03-review-send.png)

## Tips
- Keep surveys short — 5 to 8 questions usually gets the best response rates.
- Use a template the first time; you can clone and edit it later.
- Anonymous responses can encourage more candid feedback.

## Troubleshooting
- **Can’t see the New survey button** — your role may not include survey creation. Ask an admin to update your permissions.
- **Audience list is empty** — confirm the group has active members and the right segment filter applied.
