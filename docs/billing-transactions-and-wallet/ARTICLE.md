# View transactions and your wallet

> Keep tabs on every credit and cash movement in your workspace.

## Before you start
- You need a billing role to view wallet activity.
- Recent purchases can take a moment to settle — refresh if something is missing.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Billing** and select the **Wallet & transactions** tab. The header shows your current wallet and credit balances.
   ![Wallet header](./assets/01-wallet-header.png)
2. Browse the ledger below. Each row shows the date, type (top-up, charge, refund, adjustment), description, and amount. Click any row for a detailed view, including the related invoice or job.
   ![Transactions list](./assets/02-transactions-list.png)
3. Use the filters to narrow by type, date range, or status. Click **Export** to download a CSV for your finance team.

## Tips
- Check your wallet before launching a sourcing or assessment campaign so you don't run out mid-flow.
- The CSV export is great for monthly reconciliation against your invoices.

## Troubleshooting
- **Balance looks lower than expected** — open recent charges; usage-based features draw from credits as your team uses them.
- **A transaction is stuck on "pending"** — give it a few minutes. If it's still pending after 30 minutes, contact support with the transaction ID.
