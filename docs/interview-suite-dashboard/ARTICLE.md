# Use the Interview Suite dashboard

> The Interview Suite is your single view of every scheduled, live, and completed interview across the team.

## Before you start
- Open **Interviews** from the main sidebar. You see interviews you organize, attend, or have permission to view.

## Steps

1. Use the top tabs to switch between **Upcoming**, **Live now**, **Completed**, and **Cancelled**.
   ![Interview Suite tabs](./assets/01-tabs.png)
2. Filter by job, interview type, evaluator, or stage using the filter bar. Filters combine, so you can narrow to "Backend Engineer, technical, this week" in one go.
3. Click any row to open the interview detail page with the candidate brief, plan, and meeting link.
   ![Filtered list](./assets/02-filters.png)
4. Use the **Actions** menu to start a meeting, copy the join link, send a reminder, or open the scorecard.
5. Pinned at the top of the list, you will see any interviews that need your attention - missing scorecards, unconfirmed times, or pending guest evaluator responses.

## Tips
- Save your favorite filter combination as a view so you can return to it in one click.
- Hover an evaluator's avatar to see their interview load for the week.

## Troubleshooting
- **An interview is missing from the list** — check that you are on the correct tab and that no filter excludes it. Confidential interviews only show to people on the invite.
- **Live now shows nothing** — interviews appear in **Live now** five minutes before start time.
