# Update your profile and password

> Edit how you appear across Tech1m and rotate the password you use to sign in.

## Before you start
- You must be signed in.
- Have your current password ready if you plan to change it.

## Update your profile

1. Open **Settings -> Profile** from the sidebar.
   ![Profile settings entry point](./assets/01-profile-entry.png)
2. Edit your avatar, first and last name, work email, phone number, bio, role, and timezone. Each field saves when you click **Save** at the bottom.
   ![Profile fields](./assets/02-profile-fields.png)

Your timezone drives how dates and times appear in your inbox, calendar, and audit log, so keep it accurate when you travel.

## Change your password

1. Open **Settings -> Account**.
2. In the **Update Password** card, enter your current password, then a new one. The strength meter must reach a strong score before the **Update Password** button activates.
   ![Update password card](./assets/03-update-password.png)
3. Click **Update Password**. You will stay signed in on this device.

## Tips
- Pick a passphrase you do not reuse anywhere else.
- After updating, sign in again on your other devices so they pick up the new credential.

## Troubleshooting
- **Update button is disabled** - the new password is too weak. Add length or mix in symbols until the strength indicator turns green.
- **Current password is rejected** - confirm caps lock is off, then try again. If it keeps failing, sign out and use **Forgot password**.
