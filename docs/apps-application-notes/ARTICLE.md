# Add application notes

> Notes are the running record of what your team thinks about a candidate. Write them as you go — your future self and teammates will thank you.

## Before you start
- Open the candidate's application from the **Applications** tab on the job.
- Decide whether the note is for the whole hiring team or only a smaller group; you can scope it when you write it.

## Steps

1. On the candidate's profile, open the **Notes** panel.
   ![Notes panel](./assets/01-notes-panel.png)
2. Click **Add note**, type your observation, and use **@** to mention a teammate so they get notified.
   ![Add note](./assets/02-add-note.png)
3. Choose visibility — **Everyone on this hire** or a specific evaluator group — then click **Post**. The note appears with your name, time, and avatar.
   ![Post note](./assets/03-post-note.png)
4. Edit or delete your own notes from the **...** menu on the note. Other people's notes stay locked to them.

## Tips
- Keep notes short and factual ("strong on system design, weak on SQL"). Save deeper write-ups for evaluator feedback on the interview.
- Mention the hiring manager in the first note after a screen — it's the fastest way to keep them in the loop.

## Troubleshooting
- **Your @mention didn't notify anyone** — the person must be on the hiring team for this job. Add them via **Manage team** first.
- **You can't edit an old note** — notes lock after a few days to keep history honest. Add a follow-up note instead.
