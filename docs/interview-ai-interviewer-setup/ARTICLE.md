# Set up an AI interviewer

> The AI interviewer runs a structured first-round conversation with the candidate so your team only spends time on the strongest applicants.

## Before you start
- You need an interview plan with the questions you want the AI to ask.
- A scorecard template that defines the competencies the AI should rate.
- The role must allow asynchronous interviews in its hiring workflow.

## Steps

1. Open **Interviews -> Plans**, choose a plan, and toggle **Enable AI interviewer**.
   ![Enable AI interviewer](./assets/01-enable.png)
2. Pick the AI persona (formal, friendly, technical) and set the language and time limit per question.
3. Choose how the AI should react to short answers - move on, follow up once, or follow up twice.
   ![AI persona and follow-ups](./assets/02-persona.png)
4. Review the candidate-facing intro video or message. This is what they see before the interview starts.
5. Save and publish the plan. When you schedule an AI interview against this plan, the candidate gets a link to start whenever they want, and the recording, transcript, and AI insights flow back into Tech1m the moment they finish.

## Tips
- Start with one or two roles to calibrate the AI's signal scores against your team's own.
- Always keep a human follow-up round; the AI interviewer is meant to filter and surface, not decide.

## Troubleshooting
- **Toggle is greyed out** — the plan does not have a scorecard template. Attach one and try again.
- **Candidate cannot record audio** — they need to allow mic access in the browser. Tech1m runs the same speed test as live interviews before they begin.
