# Set leave and public holidays

> Define how leave works for your team, import the right public holidays, and approve time-off requests without breaking the roster.

## Before you start
- The leave types you offer (annual, sick, parental, unpaid, study, etc.).
- The countries your workers are based in — Tech1m uses this to suggest public holidays.

## Steps

1. Open **Time off** from the sidebar and click **Configure**. Add each leave type with its annual allowance, accrual rule, and whether unused days carry over.
   ![Configure leave types](./assets/01-configure-leave.png)
2. In the **Public holidays** tab, pick a country to load its official holidays for the current year. Toggle individual dates off if your business does not observe them.
   ![Public holidays per country](./assets/02-public-holidays.png)
3. Workers request time off from their workspace. New requests appear in the **Time off** table with status **Pending**.
4. Open a request to see the worker's balance, the dates, any clashes with the roster, and overlapping requests from teammates. Click **Approve** or **Decline** with an optional note.
   ![Review a leave request](./assets/03-review-request.png)
5. Approved leave is reflected on the roster and the attendance dashboard automatically — those days are excluded from absence counts.

## Tips
- Use **Add time off** to record leave on a worker's behalf — useful for sickness called in by phone.
- The worker sees their remaining balance per leave type in their workspace, so you do not have to answer that question manually.

## Troubleshooting
- **Public holidays did not import** — the country was not selected, or the year has not been published yet. Re-pick the country and confirm the year.
- **A worker has a negative balance** — they took more leave than their accrual allows. Adjust the allowance or convert the excess to unpaid leave.
