# Configure surveys

> Set the defaults that every new survey will inherit so your team doesn’t reconfigure them each time.

## Before you start
- You need an admin or workspace-owner role to change these settings.
- Have your branding assets (logo, accent colour) ready if you plan to update them.

## Steps

1. Open **Engagement Centre** and click the **Settings** icon in the top right.
   ![Engagement settings entry](./assets/01-settings-entry.png)
2. Under **General**, set the default sender name, reply-to email, and survey time zone.
3. Open **Branding** to upload your logo and pick the colours that appear on survey pages and emails.
   ![Branding panel](./assets/02-branding.png)
4. In **Reminders**, choose how many follow-ups recipients get and how far apart they’re sent.
5. Use **Anonymity & privacy** to decide which question types can be anonymous and what minimum response count is required before results are shown.
6. Click **Save changes**. Existing surveys keep their original settings — new surveys pick up the new defaults.
   ![Save confirmation](./assets/03-save-confirm.png)

## Tips
- Set a minimum response threshold (for example, 5) to protect anonymity in small teams.
- Test reminders by sending a survey to yourself before rolling out to everyone.

## Troubleshooting
- **Changes don’t appear on a survey** — defaults only apply to new surveys; edit individual surveys to update them.
- **Logo looks blurry** — upload a square PNG of at least 512×512px.
