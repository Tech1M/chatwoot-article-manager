# Reschedule or cancel an interview

> Move an interview to a new time or call it off without breaking the candidate experience.

## Before you start
- You need edit access to the interview (the organizer, a hiring manager on the job, or an admin).
- For reschedules, confirm any new evaluators have a connected calendar.

## Steps

1. Open the interview from the **Interview Suite** dashboard or the candidate's application.
   ![Interview detail page](./assets/01-detail-page.png)
2. Click **Reschedule** to pick a new time, or **Cancel interview** to call it off.
3. For reschedule, choose a new slot from the availability grid. You can also swap evaluators here.
   ![Reschedule dialog](./assets/02-reschedule.png)
4. Add a short note to explain the change. This appears in the email and on the timeline.
5. Choose who to notify (candidate, evaluators, or both) and click **Confirm**.

## Tips
- Reschedules keep the same interview ID, so scorecards, recordings, and notes stay attached.
- Cancellations are recorded on the timeline with the reason you provided. Use it for audit and analytics.

## Troubleshooting
- **Calendar invite still shows the old time** — ask the recipient to refresh; some clients cache for a few minutes. If the issue persists, resend from the interview page.
- **Cannot cancel a completed interview** — you can only archive completed sessions, not cancel them.
