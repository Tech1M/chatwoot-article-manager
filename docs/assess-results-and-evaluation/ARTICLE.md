# Review results and evaluate

> Every submitted assessment lands in your dashboard with auto-scored sections graded for you. Your job is to read the rest and make a call.

## Before you start
- A candidate who has completed the assessment.
- Your team's rubric for what "passing" looks like for this role.

## Steps

1. Open the assessment and go to **Sessions**. Click any completed session to see the candidate's full submission.
   ![Sessions list](./assets/01-sessions-list.png)
2. Review each section. Multiple-choice and code-challenge sections are auto-scored; written answers and take-home submissions need a human reviewer.
   ![Section breakdown](./assets/02-section-breakdown.png)
3. Add your **score and notes** per section. If AI evaluation is enabled, you'll see a suggested score you can accept, adjust, or override.
   ![Reviewer scoring panel](./assets/03-reviewer-scoring.png)
4. Mark the session **Passed**, **Failed**, or **Needs review** — this updates the candidate's pipeline stage if the assessment is wired to a job.

## Tips
- Calibrate with another reviewer on the first few sessions per role to keep scoring consistent.
- Use the **Compare** view to view two candidates side-by-side on the same questions.

## Troubleshooting
- **Score looks wrong on a code challenge** — open the test-case detail to see which cases failed and why.
- **Can't change a final decision** — once shared with the candidate, decisions are locked. Reopen the session from its menu to edit.
