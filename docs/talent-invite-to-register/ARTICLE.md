# Invite candidates to register

> Move a candidate from "name on a list" to a verified Tech1m profile so they can apply, take assessments, and book interviews.

## Before you start
- The candidate must have at least an email address on their profile.
- Make sure their basic details (name, current role) are accurate — they will see them on the invite.

## Steps

1. Open the candidate's profile from any pool or search result.
   ![Candidate profile](./assets/01-candidate-profile.png)
2. Click **Invite to register**. You can also do this in bulk by selecting candidates in a pool and choosing **Invite to register** from the action bar.
   ![Invite to register](./assets/02-invite-to-register.png)
3. Optionally personalize the invite message and pick a job to associate the invite with. Leave the job blank for a general invitation.
   ![Customize invite](./assets/03-customize-invite.png)
4. Click **Send invite**. The candidate receives an email with a secure link to claim their profile.
   ![Invite sent](./assets/04-invite-sent.png)

## Tips
- Once a candidate registers, the existing profile data is merged into their new account, and they can keep building it themselves.
- Track invite status from the candidate's activity tab — you will see when they open the email and when they finish registering.
- Resend the invite from the same button if a week has passed and the candidate has not signed up.

## Troubleshooting
- **Invite button is disabled** — the candidate has no email address. Add one and try again.
- **Candidate says they did not get the email** — check the spelling of the email and ask them to check spam. You can also resend or share the direct link.
