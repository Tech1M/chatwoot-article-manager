# Comment and react on the Feed

> Reactions are the fastest way to acknowledge a post; comments let you add context, ask follow-ups, or celebrate publicly.

## Before you start
- Reactions and comments respect each post's settings — the author may have disabled either one.
- Everyone in the post's visibility scope can see your comments and reactions.

## Steps

1. Hover over a post and pick a reaction from the strip — like, celebrate, support, and more.
   ![Reactions strip](./assets/01-reactions.png)
2. Click **Comment** to open the reply box. Comments support text, mentions, and emoji.
   ![Add a comment](./assets/02-comment.png)
3. Mention a teammate by typing **@** and choosing their name. They get a notification with a link straight to the comment.
   ![Mention teammate](./assets/03-mention.png)

## Tips
- A reaction is often enough — save comments for when you have something specific to add.
- The latest comments preview under each post; click **View all** to see the full thread.

## Troubleshooting
- **No reaction strip** — the author turned reactions off for that post.
- **Mention didn't notify** — the person may not have access to the post's audience, so they can't be tagged on it.
