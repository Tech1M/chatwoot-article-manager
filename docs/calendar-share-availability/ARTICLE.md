# Share your availability

> Pick a few open slots from your calendar and send them to a candidate or guest. They choose one and Tech1m books the meeting.

## Before you start
- A connected calendar so the slots reflect your real schedule.
- The email of the person you're sharing with (candidate, guest evaluator, partner).

## Steps

1. Open the calendar and click **Share availability**. Set the date range, the meeting length, and how many slots you want to offer.
   ![Share availability setup](./assets/01-share-setup.png)
2. Tech1m surfaces open slots from your connected calendars. Pick the ones you're happy to offer — uncheck any you'd rather hold.
   ![Pick slots to share](./assets/02-pick-slots.png)
3. Add the recipient and a short message, then send. They get a link, choose a time, and the meeting is added to both calendars automatically.
   ![Send availability link](./assets/03-send-link.png)

## Tips
- Offer 3–5 slots across different days — too few and you'll play tag, too many and recipients freeze.
- Once a recipient picks a slot, the others release back to your calendar so they don't stay blocked.

## Troubleshooting
- **The recipient says all slots are taken** — your calendar likely filled up after you sent the link. Send a fresh set.
- **The booked meeting isn't on your calendar** — resync from **Settings → Calendars**; the event should appear within a minute.
- **Times look wrong to the recipient** — Tech1m shows times in their time zone automatically. If yours looks off, check the time zone on your profile.
