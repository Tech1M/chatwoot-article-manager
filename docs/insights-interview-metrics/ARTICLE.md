# Interview metrics

> Measure how your interview loop is performing so you can fix slow scheduling, missed sessions, or thin scorecards.

## Before you start
- Schedule interviews through Tech1m so they appear in the metrics.
- Ask interviewers to submit scorecards after each session.

## Steps

1. Open **Insights**, then click the **Interviews** tab.
   ![Interviews tab](./assets/01-interviews-tab.png)
2. Pick a date range and, optionally, a job or interviewer to focus the view.
3. Review the headline cards: total interviews, average lead time, completion rate, and scorecard submission rate.
   ![Headline metric cards](./assets/02-headline.png)
4. Scroll to **Interviewer load** to see who is over- or under-booked. Hover a bar for hours spent and average scorecard turnaround.
5. Open the **No-shows and reschedules** section to see which jobs and stages lose the most time, with a list of recent affected interviews.
   ![No-show breakdown](./assets/03-no-shows.png)

## Tips
- Set an internal target for scorecard submission within 24 hours and watch the rate trend.
- Use interviewer load to balance assignments before opening new requisitions.

## Troubleshooting
- **Interviews missing** — they were not scheduled through Tech1m or were created on a calendar that is not connected.
- **Scorecard rate is 0%** — confirm interviewers are using the Tech1m scorecard, not a separate doc.
