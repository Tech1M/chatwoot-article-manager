# Create your business account

> Spin up a brand-new Tech1m workspace for your company in a few minutes.

## Before you start
- A work email address you can access right now.
- Your company name and country.

## Steps

1. Go to the Tech1m sign-up page and choose **Create a business account**.
   ![Sign-up landing page](./assets/01-signup-landing.png)
2. Enter your name, work email, and a strong password. Accept the terms to continue.
   ![Sign-up form](./assets/02-signup-form.png)
3. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to confirm your address.
   ![Verification email](./assets/03-verify-email.png)
4. Tell us a little about your company — name, size, and what you mostly hire for. This shapes the templates and recommendations you see next.
   ![Onboarding questions](./assets/04-onboarding.png)

When onboarding finishes, you land on your workspace dashboard as the workspace owner.

## Tips
- Use a shared work email (like `talent@yourcompany.com`) if you want the workspace owner role to outlive any one person.
- You can rename the workspace later in **Settings → Company**.

## Troubleshooting
- **Verification email never arrives** — check spam, then use **Resend verification** on the sign-in screen. If it still does not show up, contact support.
- **"Email already in use"** — someone at your company may already have a workspace. Ask them to invite you instead of starting a new one.
