# Get candidate recommendations for a job

> Turn a job posting into a sourced candidate shortlist without writing the search yourself.

## Before you start
- The job needs a clear title, required skills, and location filled in — recommendations are only as good as the inputs.
- You need permission to view AI Sourcing.

## Steps

1. Open the job, then go to the **Source candidates** tab.
   ![Source candidates tab on a job](./assets/01-source-tab.png)
2. Tech1m reads the role, skills, and location from the job and runs a sourcing search for you. Review the recommended candidates.
   ![Recommended sourced candidates](./assets/02-recommendations.png)
3. Refine the recommendations using the filter bar — add seniority, company type, or country to focus the list. Save the result as a saved search if you'll come back to it.
4. Click a candidate to open their profile, then add them to a talent pool or enroll them directly into an engagement sequence tied to this role.

## Tips
- For better recommendations, keep the job's required skills tight — five strong skills beat fifteen vague ones.
- Use this alongside the **Curated** tab on the job: curated suggests candidates already in your pool, while sourcing suggests new people.

## Troubleshooting
- **The list looks off-target** — the job description may be too generic. Update the title and required skills, then refresh.
- **Nothing comes back** — the location or skill combination is too narrow. Drop one filter and try again.
