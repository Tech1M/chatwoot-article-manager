# Archive or delete a conversation

> Archive a conversation when it's done but you may want it for reference. Delete one when the content shouldn't stick around.

## Before you start
- Archiving hides a conversation from your active list but keeps every message. You can restore it any time.
- Deleting is permanent for the recipient and shouldn't be used to hide compliance-relevant exchanges.

## Steps

1. Open the conversation in your **Inbox** and click the menu icon in the header.
   ![Conversation menu](./assets/01-menu.png)
2. Choose **Archive conversation** to move it out of your active list. To bring it back later, switch the Inbox filter to **Archived** and click **Restore**.
   ![Archive option](./assets/02-archive.png)
3. To remove a thread entirely, choose **Delete conversation** and confirm. This is irreversible.
   ![Delete confirmation](./assets/03-delete.png)

## Tips
- Archive after a candidate is hired or rejected to keep your active Inbox focused on live conversations.
- Use search across both active and archived to find any past message — archiving doesn't break search.

## Troubleshooting
- **Archive option is greyed out** — only the conversation owner can archive. Ask the teammate who started it.
- **Restored a conversation but it looks empty** — give it a moment to reload; messages are kept and will reappear.
