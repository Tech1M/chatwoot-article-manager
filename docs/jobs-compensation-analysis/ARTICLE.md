# Run a compensation analysis

> Benchmark your salary range against the market in a couple of clicks.

## Before you start
- Have your proposed salary range and currency ready.
- The analysis uses the job's title, location, and seniority — make sure those are accurate.

## Steps

1. While editing a job, open the compensation section and click **Run analysis**.
   ![Run analysis button](./assets/01-run-button.png)
2. The result shows the market range for similar roles — low, median, and high — alongside your proposed range.
   ![Market comparison chart](./assets/02-comparison.png)
3. If your range sits below market, adjust it or click **Apply suggested range** to use the median.
   ![Apply suggestion](./assets/03-apply.png)
4. Save the job to lock in the new range.

## Tips
- Tight ranges signal confidence and save back-and-forth — try not to span more than 25%.
- Re-run the analysis every 6 months for evergreen roles; markets shift.

## Troubleshooting
- **No data returned** — very niche roles or rare locations may lack benchmarks. Use a closer city or more general title.
- **Suggested range looks wrong** — confirm the seniority and employment type fields. Wrong inputs skew the benchmark.
