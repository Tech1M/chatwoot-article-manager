# Manage your question bank

> Build a reusable library of interview questions so every evaluator on your team draws from the same well.

## Before you start
- Admins and hiring managers can edit the bank. Other evaluators can browse it.
- Decide on the competencies you want to tag against (for example, system design, ownership, communication).

## Steps

1. Open **Interviews -> Question bank** from the sidebar.
   ![Question bank list](./assets/01-bank.png)
2. Click **Add question**. Enter the prompt, an ideal answer outline, and tag the competency, role, difficulty, and interview stage.
3. Use folders to group questions by job family or interview type.
   ![Add a question](./assets/02-add-question.png)
4. To edit, click any question. Edits propagate to interview plans that reference it; one-off forks stay local to that plan.
5. Use the search and filters to find a question fast. Bulk select to move, archive, or export.

## Tips
- Mark sensitive or hazing questions as **Restricted** to keep them out of casual use.
- Pin go-to questions per role so new interviewers find them immediately.

## Troubleshooting
- **Cannot add a question** — your role does not have edit permission on the bank. Ask an admin.
- **A question shows up in the wrong folder** — open the question and update its tags; folders are tag-driven.
