# Place bulk orders

> Use a benefit plan to send several gift cards to one team member in a single, auditable transaction.

## Before you start
- Build a benefit plan that groups the perks you want to fulfil together. (See your benefits admin if you don't have one yet.)
- Add a payment method that has enough balance or limit to cover the total.

## Steps

1. Open **Perks** and choose the team member you're fulfilling for. Click **Fulfil benefit plan**.
   ![Choose team member](./assets/01-choose-team-member.png)
2. Pick the benefit plan from the dropdown. The panel shows every gift card included and the combined total.
   ![Review plan](./assets/02-review-plan.png)
3. Choose your payment method and click **Place orders**. Each gift card in the plan is ordered as its own line so you can track and refund individually if needed. The recipient gets one email per card as they're delivered.

## Tips
- Bulk orders show up as a group in your order history, so you can filter to one fulfilment event when reconciling.
- If a single card in the plan fails, the others still go through. You can retry just the failed one.

## Troubleshooting
- **Some cards failed** — open each failed order to see the merchant reason and retry. You're only charged for successful orders.
- **The total looks wrong** — check the currency on each perk in the plan; mixed currencies are converted at checkout.
