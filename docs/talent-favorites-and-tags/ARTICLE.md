# Favorite and tag candidates

> Keep your strongest candidates easy to find and label everyone in a way that works for your team.

## Before you start
- Open a talent pool with candidates in it.
- Decide on a small, consistent set of tags before tagging at scale — for example, "shortlist", "passive", "senior", "remote-only".

## Steps

1. Hover over a candidate row and click the **star** to mark them as a favorite. The star turns gold and they will appear when you filter by favorites.
   ![Favorite a candidate](./assets/01-favorite-star.png)
2. To tag, click a candidate to open their profile, then click **Add tag** in the tags section.
   ![Add tag](./assets/02-add-tag.png)
3. Pick an existing tag from the suggestions, or type a new tag name and press Enter to create it.
   ![Choose tag](./assets/03-choose-tag.png)
4. To remove a tag, click the small "x" on the tag pill.
   ![Remove tag](./assets/04-remove-tag.png)

## Tips
- Use favorites for "I want to come back to this person", and tags for facts about the person ("React", "Lagos", "Open to relocation").
- You can manage all your tags from the **Tags** section of Talent CRM settings — rename, merge, or delete them there.
- Tags and favorites both work as filters on the pool list.

## Troubleshooting
- **A new tag did not save** — make sure you pressed Enter or clicked outside the tag input to commit the change.
- **My teammate cannot see a tag** — tags are shared across the company. Have them refresh the page.
