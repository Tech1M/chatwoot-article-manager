# View referral payments

> See what you've been paid, what's ready to redeem, and what's still on the way.

## Before you start
- You need at least one qualified referral with a reward earned.
- Make sure your payout details are saved in your billing profile — payments can't go out without them.

## Steps

1. Open **Refer & earn** and switch to the **Payments** tab. The summary shows your total paid, available balance, and pending balance.
   ![Payments summary](./assets/01-payments-summary.png)
2. Browse the payments list to see each payout — date, amount, method, and status (processing, paid, or failed). Click a row for the full breakdown of which rewards it covered.
   ![Payments list](./assets/02-payments-list.png)
3. When your available balance hits the minimum payout threshold, click **Request payout**. You'll see the request appear in the list as *processing* until it clears.

## Tips
- Set up your payout details early so rewards land faster once they qualify.
- Wait until a few rewards stack up before requesting a payout — it cuts down on fees and admin.

## Troubleshooting
- **Request payout button is disabled** — your available balance is below the minimum threshold, or your payout details are missing.
- **A payment failed** — most failures are caused by outdated payout details. Update them and retry from the failed row.
