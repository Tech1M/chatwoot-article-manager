# Send SMS to candidates

> Get higher reply rates than email by texting candidates straight from their profile.

## Before you start
- The candidate must have a valid phone number on their profile.
- Your company must have SMS credits available — check your billing wallet if you are unsure.

## Steps

1. Open a candidate profile and click **Send SMS**, or select multiple candidates in a pool and choose **SMS** from the bulk action bar.
   ![Send SMS button](./assets/01-send-sms.png)
2. Pick a message template, or write a fresh message. You can use placeholders like `{{first_name}}` to personalize messages at scale.
   ![Compose SMS](./assets/02-compose-sms.png)
3. Review the preview. Tech1m shows the character count and the number of SMS segments that will be billed per recipient.
   ![SMS preview](./assets/03-sms-preview.png)
4. Click **Send**. The message is recorded on each candidate's activity timeline and conversation thread.
   ![SMS sent](./assets/04-sms-sent.png)

## Tips
- Keep messages short, friendly, and identify yourself and your company in the first line.
- Replies appear in the candidate's conversation thread inside Talent CRM, so you can keep chatting in the same place.
- Avoid sending late at night — schedule for working hours where the candidate lives.

## Troubleshooting
- **Send button is disabled** — the candidate has no valid phone number, or your wallet is out of SMS credits.
- **Message failed for some recipients** — open the activity tab to see the carrier error. Fix the number or try again later.
