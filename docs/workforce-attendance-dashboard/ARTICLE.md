# Use the attendance dashboard

> Get a live view of who is working today and act on lateness, no-shows, or unexpected absences before they become a problem.

## Before you start
- A published roster with shifts assigned for today.
- Workers have clocked in via their workspace, the mobile app, or a kiosk.

## Steps

1. Open **Attendance** from the sidebar. The header shows totals for **Scheduled**, **On time**, **Late**, **Absent**, and **On leave**.
   ![Attendance header](./assets/01-attendance-header.png)
2. Use the date selector to switch between today, yesterday, or a custom range. Filter by team, site, or shift type to narrow the table.
3. Open a row to see the timeline — clock-in, breaks, clock-out, and any flagged events such as early leave or geofence mismatch.
   ![Attendance row detail](./assets/02-attendance-detail.png)
4. Use the **Intelligence panel** on the right for trends — recurring lateness, attendance score by team, and workers who need a check-in.
5. Click **Adjust** on a record to correct a missed clock-in or fix a wrong shift. Adjustments are logged with your name and reason for audit.

## Tips
- Pin the **Late** and **Absent** filters as a tab so the dashboard opens straight to what needs your attention.
- Export the day's attendance to CSV for payroll reconciliation at the end of the pay period.

## Troubleshooting
- **A worker shows as absent but they say they clocked in** — check their device location and the shift's geofence. Adjust the record manually if needed.
- **Numbers do not match the roster** — the roster was changed after publishing. Republish to refresh the dashboard.
