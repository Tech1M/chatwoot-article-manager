# Distribute an assessment and invite candidates

> Once your assessment is published, you can wire it into a job stage, invite candidates one-by-one, or share a single link.

## Before you start
- A published assessment.
- A list of candidates, or a pipeline stage that should auto-trigger the assessment.

## Steps

1. From the assessment overview, click **Distribute**. Pick a method: **Job stage**, **Direct invite**, or **Public link**.
   ![Distribute options](./assets/01-distribute-options.png)
2. For job stages, choose the job and the pipeline stage. Anyone moved into that stage will receive the assessment automatically.
   ![Job stage selection](./assets/02-job-stage.png)
3. For direct invites, paste candidate emails (or pick from your talent pool), customize the invite message, and click **Send**. Each candidate gets a unique link.
   ![Direct invite composer](./assets/03-direct-invite.png)
4. Track every invite under the assessment's **Sessions** tab — see who started, who finished, and who hasn't opened it yet.

## Tips
- Set an expiry date on invites so abandoned sessions don't pile up.
- Public links are convenient but anyone with the link can attempt — use them only for top-of-funnel screening.

## Troubleshooting
- **Candidate didn't get the email** — check the Sessions tab for delivery status, then ask them to check spam. You can resend from the same row.
- **Auto-trigger didn't fire** — make sure the assessment is published and the job stage is configured to send it.
