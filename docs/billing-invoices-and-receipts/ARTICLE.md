# Find invoices and receipts

> Grab any invoice or receipt for your finance team in just a few clicks.

## Before you start
- You need a billing role to access invoices.
- Receipts are also emailed to your billing contacts after each charge — check there first if you only need the latest one.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Billing** and select the **Invoices** tab. You'll see every invoice ordered by date, with status, amount, and what it covered.
   ![Invoices list](./assets/01-invoices-list.png)
2. Use the search box or date filter to narrow the list — handy at month-end or for an audit.
3. Click any row to preview the invoice. Use **Download PDF** to save it, or **Email** to send it to a teammate.
   ![Invoice detail](./assets/02-invoice-detail.png)

## Tips
- Add finance teammates as billing contacts so invoices arrive in their inbox automatically.
- The PDF includes your registered company details. Keep your billing profile up to date if those details change.

## Troubleshooting
- **An invoice is missing** — only paid charges show up here. Pending or failed attempts appear under **Wallet & transactions** instead.
- **Wrong company details on the PDF** — update your billing profile in **Settings → Billing → Billing profile**, then re-download.
