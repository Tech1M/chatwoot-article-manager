# Upgrade job distribution

> Get more eyes on a role by adding paid distribution channels on top of your standard listing.

## Before you start
- The job must already be created and published.
- You need a billing role to confirm the upgrade payment.

## Steps

1. Open the job you want to promote and click **Distribution**, or open **Settings → Billing → Distribution** to start from there.
   ![Distribution tab on a job](./assets/01-distribution-tab.png)
2. Pick the upgrade tier or individual channels you want to add. The estimated reach and price update as you select.
   ![Choose distribution upgrade](./assets/02-choose-upgrade.png)
3. Review the summary and click **Confirm upgrade**. Distribution kicks off automatically once payment is approved, and you'll see the new channels listed under the job.
   ![Confirm distribution upgrade](./assets/03-confirm-upgrade.png)

## Tips
- Upgrade soon after publishing — early visibility usually drives the best applicant flow.
- You can stack upgrades later if a role needs more reach.

## Troubleshooting
- **Upgrade options are greyed out** — the job is paused or closed. Reopen it before upgrading.
- **The job still shows the old reach** — give the channels a few minutes to publish. Some partner boards can take up to an hour.
