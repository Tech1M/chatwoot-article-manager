# Use smart event scheduling

> Type something like "30-minute intro with Ada next Tuesday afternoon" and Tech1m will find a slot, draft the event, and add it to your calendar.

## Before you start
- A connected calendar so Tech1m knows when you're busy.
- The email addresses of any teammates or candidates you want to invite.

## Steps

1. From the calendar, click **Smart add**. Describe the event in your own words — title, who should attend, rough time, and duration.
   ![Smart add input](./assets/01-smart-add.png)
2. Review the proposed event. Tech1m fills in the title, attendees, time, and a starter description, and shows the slot it picked from your free time.
   ![Smart add proposal](./assets/02-proposal.png)
3. Adjust anything that's off, then click **Create event**. The event is added to your default connected calendar and invitations go out to attendees.
   ![Event added confirmation](./assets/03-event-added.png)

## Tips
- Mention a duration ("45 minutes") and a window ("Thursday morning") for the best results.
- For interviews, start scheduling from the candidate's profile instead — Tech1m pre-fills the candidate, role, and interview plan.

## Troubleshooting
- **No slot was found** — your free time window might be too tight. Widen the date range, or remove an attendee with no overlap.
- **Wrong attendee picked up** — clear the attendee field and type the email directly.
- **Event didn't appear in Google Calendar** — open **Settings → Calendars** and resync. The event should land within a minute.
