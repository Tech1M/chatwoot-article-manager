# Build a custom report

> Slice your hiring data exactly the way you want and save it for repeat use.

## Before you start
- You need the **Create reports** permission.
- Know which question you want to answer — it makes column choices much easier.

## Steps

1. Go to **Reports** from the sidebar and click **New report**.
   ![New report button](./assets/01-new-report.png)
2. Pick a **data source**: jobs, applications, interviews, hires, or activity. The available columns and filters depend on your choice.
3. Add the **columns** you want to see. Drag to reorder them.
   ![Column picker](./assets/02-columns.png)
4. Add **filters** — for example, status is "Hired" and start date is in this quarter. Combine filters with AND/OR logic.
5. Optionally pick a **group by** field (recruiter, department, source) and a chart type to visualize the result.
6. Click **Run** to preview, then **Save**. Give it a clear name and decide whether the report is private or shared with your workspace.
   ![Save report dialog](./assets/03-save.png)

## Tips
- Start from a template under **Reports → Templates** when one is close to what you need.
- Saved reports can be scheduled to email a refreshed copy on a cadence.

## Troubleshooting
- **No rows returned** — your filters are likely too narrow; remove one and try again.
- **Cannot save** — the name is empty or already used in your workspace.
