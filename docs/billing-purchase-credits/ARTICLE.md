# Purchase credits

> Add credits to your workspace so your team can keep using usage-based features without interruption.

## Before you start
- You need a billing role to make a purchase.
- Add a payment method first if you haven't already — you'll be asked for one at checkout.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Billing** and select the **Plan & credits** tab. Your current credit balance is shown at the top.
   ![Credit balance](./assets/01-credit-balance.png)
2. Click **Buy credits**. Choose one of the suggested credit packs, or enter a custom amount.
   ![Choose credit pack](./assets/02-credit-pack.png)
3. Review the total, confirm your payment method, and click **Pay now**. Credits land in your wallet within a few seconds and a receipt is emailed to your billing contacts.
   ![Confirm purchase](./assets/03-confirm-purchase.png)

## Tips
- Larger packs come with a better rate — check the price-per-credit shown next to each option.
- Turn on **Auto top-up** to refill automatically whenever your balance drops below a threshold you set.

## Troubleshooting
- **Payment didn't go through** — your bank may have declined the charge. Try a different card or see [My payment failed](../faq-billing-failed-payment/ARTICLE.md).
- **Credits didn't appear** — give it a minute and refresh. If they still aren't there, check the **Wallet & transactions** tab to confirm the purchase landed before contacting support.
