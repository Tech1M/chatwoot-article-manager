# Review applications

> Open the applications list for a job, filter to what you need to look at, and walk through each candidate's profile.

## Before you start
- Make sure you have access to the job. If you can see it in **Jobs**, you can review its applications.
- Have a sense of what you're looking for — must-have skills, location, or specific screening answers — so you can decide quickly.

## Steps

1. From the sidebar, open **Jobs** and click the job you want to review.
   ![Job list](./assets/01-job-list.png)
2. Open the **Applications** tab. New applications land in the **Applied** stage by default.
   ![Applications tab](./assets/02-applications-tab.png)
3. Use the stage tabs or filters to narrow the list — for example, only show **Applied** candidates you haven't touched yet, or filter by location, source, or score.
4. Click any candidate to open their full profile. You'll see their resume, screening answers, AI fit summary, and any prior activity.
   ![Candidate profile](./assets/03-candidate-profile.png)
5. From the profile, take action: rate, tag, leave a note, move them to the next stage, or reject. Use the next/previous arrows to keep moving through the list without going back.

## Tips
- The **Pending review** filter hides anyone you (or a teammate) has already actioned, so you can focus on fresh applicants.
- Sort by **AI score** to bring the strongest matches to the top, then scan the rest manually.

## Troubleshooting
- **The list is empty** — check your filters; the stage tab or "pending review" toggle may be hiding everyone.
- **You can't open a candidate** — your role may be limited to a department or job. Ask an admin to grant access.
