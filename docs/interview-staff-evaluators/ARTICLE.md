# Manage staff evaluators

> Decide who on your team can interview, when they are available, and which roles they should be paired with.

## Before you start
- You need admin permissions on the workspace to manage evaluator settings.
- Staff need active accounts in Tech1m before they can be added.

## Steps

1. Open **Interviews -> Evaluators -> Staff**. You will see every staff member with interview permissions and their current load.
   ![Staff evaluators list](./assets/01-staff-list.png)
2. Click **Add evaluator** to grant interview rights to another team member, or open an existing row to update them.
3. Set their interview types (phone screen, technical, panel, etc.), the roles they cover, and any weekly load cap.
   ![Evaluator settings](./assets/02-settings.png)
4. Open the **Availability** tab to confirm their connected calendar and to set buffer times before and after interviews.
5. Save. From now on, scheduling will respect their load, role coverage, and availability.

## Tips
- Use load caps to protect senior staff from interview overload.
- Tag specialists (for example, security, ML) so the scheduler can find them quickly.

## Troubleshooting
- **Evaluator never appears as available** — they have not connected a calendar. Ask them to connect Google or Zoho from their profile.
- **Wrong types showing** — open their evaluator profile and adjust the interview types.
