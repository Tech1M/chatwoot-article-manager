# Submit and approve milestones

> Learn how contractors submit work for a milestone and how your team approves or requests revisions before payment is released.

## Before you start
- The contract that contains the milestone has been signed.
- The contractor knows which milestone they are working against.
- A reviewer with milestone approval rights is assigned on your side.

## Steps

1. Open the contractor's contract from **Operations → Milestones** and pick the milestone you want to track.
   ![Milestones list with the active milestone highlighted](./assets/01-milestones-list.png)
2. When the contractor submits, they attach their deliverables and a short note. You will see the submission appear under the milestone with a **Pending review** badge.
   ![Pending submission card on the milestone detail page](./assets/02-pending-submission.png)
3. Open the submission, review the attachments and the contractor's note, and choose **Approve** to move the milestone forward, or **Request revisions** to send it back with feedback.
   ![Approve and request revisions actions on the submission](./assets/03-approve-or-revise.png)
4. If you request revisions, the milestone returns to the contractor with your comments. They resubmit and you repeat the review.

## Tips
- Keep revision feedback specific — point to the file or section that needs change.
- Use the milestone conversation to ask questions before rejecting outright.

## Troubleshooting
- **The Approve button is disabled** — the milestone needs to be funded first; see Fund and pay milestones.
- **Contractor cannot submit** — confirm the contract is active and the previous milestone is closed.
