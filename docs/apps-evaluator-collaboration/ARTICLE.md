# Collaborate with evaluators

> Evaluators are the people who help you decide — interviewers, hiring managers, technical reviewers. Add them to a job so they only see what they need.

## Before you start
- The evaluator must already be a team member. If they're not, invite them from **Settings → Team** first.
- Decide whether they're reviewing all candidates on a job or only the ones assigned to them.

## Steps

1. Open the job and click **Hiring team** (or **Manage team**) on the job header.
   ![Hiring team](./assets/01-hiring-team.png)
2. Click **Add evaluator**, pick the teammate, and choose their role — for example, **Interviewer** or **Hiring manager**. Their access is scoped to this job only.
   ![Add evaluator](./assets/02-add-evaluator.png)
3. Optionally assign them to specific candidates or interview stages so they see a focused list when they log in.
4. The evaluator now sees the job in their dashboard. They can rate, tag, leave notes, and submit interview feedback. Mention them with **@** in any note to nudge them.
   ![Evaluator view](./assets/03-evaluator-view.png)
5. Track their input on the candidate's profile: ratings show next to their name, and feedback appears in the **Evaluations** panel after each interview.

## Tips
- Pull evaluator ratings into your debrief — the team average is shown at the top of the candidate, but individual scores often tell the story.
- Remove an evaluator from the **Hiring team** panel when their part is done so notifications stop and access is tidy.

## Troubleshooting
- **The evaluator can't see the job** — confirm they were added to *this* job's hiring team, not just the company. Job access is per-job.
- **They got too many notifications** — they can mute the job from their notification settings without losing access.
