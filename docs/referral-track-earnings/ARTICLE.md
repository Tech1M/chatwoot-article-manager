# Track your referral earnings

> Watch your referral funnel in real time, from first click to qualified reward.

## Before you start
- You need at least one referral link. See [Create a referral link](../referral-create-link/ARTICLE.md) if you haven't made one yet.
- New activity can take a few minutes to appear on the dashboard.

## Steps

1. Open **Refer & earn** from the sidebar. The top cards summarise total clicks, sign-ups, qualified referrals, and rewards earned.
   ![Referral summary cards](./assets/01-summary-cards.png)
2. Scroll to the **Leads** table to see each person who clicked or signed up, the link they used, and where they are in the funnel — pending, qualified, or rewarded.
   ![Referral leads table](./assets/02-leads-table.png)
3. Switch to the **Rewards** tab to see each reward you've earned, what triggered it, and its current status (pending, available, or paid).
   ![Rewards tab](./assets/03-rewards-tab.png)

## Tips
- Filter by link or date range to compare which campaigns convert best.
- Rewards usually move from *pending* to *available* once the referred workspace meets the qualifying activity in the program terms.

## Troubleshooting
- **A sign-up isn't showing** — the referred workspace may have signed up without using your link, or used a different one. Attribution sticks to the last referral link clicked.
- **A reward stays pending too long** — qualifying activity can take time. Check the program terms for the timeline before reaching out.
