# Schedule an interview

> Book a new interview with a candidate, attach evaluators, and send confirmations in a few clicks.

## Before you start
- The candidate must already have an application against an open job.
- At least one connected calendar (Google or Zoho) for the host evaluator.
- Optional: a saved interview plan template for the role.

## Steps

1. Open the Interview Suite from the sidebar and click **Schedule interview** in the top right.
   ![Schedule interview button](./assets/01-schedule-button.png)
2. Pick the candidate and the job application. The role and pipeline stage fill in automatically.
3. Choose an interview type (phone screen, technical, panel, final, or AI interview) and an interview plan if you have one.
   ![Choose interview type and plan](./assets/02-type-and-plan.png)
4. Add evaluators. Tech1m shows their availability side by side based on the connected calendars.
5. Pick a time slot, set duration, time zone, and the meeting location or video link. Tech1m auto-generates a Zoom or Google Meet link if you leave it blank.
   ![Pick a time slot](./assets/03-time-slot.png)
6. Review the candidate-facing email and click **Send invites**. Calendar invites go to evaluators and the candidate at the same time.

## Tips
- Use **Suggest times** to let Tech1m propose three open slots that work for every required evaluator.
- Set the interview to **Confidential** if hiring managers should not see it on the public schedule.

## Troubleshooting
- **No availability shown for an evaluator** — they have not connected a calendar yet. See [Manage staff evaluators](../interview-staff-evaluators/ARTICLE.md).
- **Candidate did not receive the invite** — check the application email is verified, then resend from the interview detail page.
