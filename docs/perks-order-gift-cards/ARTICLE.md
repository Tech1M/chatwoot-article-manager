# Order gift cards

> Reward a team member with a gift card from a wide catalogue of merchants — delivered straight to their inbox.

## Before you start
- Add a payment method in **Billing** so we can charge the order.
- Make sure the recipient is a team member or talent already on your account.

## Steps

1. Open **Perks → Gift cards**. Browse the catalogue or use the search and filters to narrow by merchant or category.
   ![Gift card catalogue](./assets/01-gift-card-catalogue.png)
2. Click a card to open the order panel. Pick the recipient, currency, and the amount you want to load. The total updates as you change the value.
   ![Order panel](./assets/02-order-panel.png)
3. Choose your payment method, review the summary, and click **Place order**. The recipient gets an email with the gift card details once it's delivered.

## Tips
- Some merchants have fixed denominations — if so, the panel will only let you pick from those values.
- Encourage the recipient to redeem promptly. Many cards have an expiry date set by the merchant, shown on the order.

## Troubleshooting
- **Order shows as failed** — the merchant rejected the request (often a stock issue). Try a different value or merchant; you won't be charged for failed orders.
- **Recipient didn't get the email** — ask them to check spam, then open the order and resend the delivery email.
