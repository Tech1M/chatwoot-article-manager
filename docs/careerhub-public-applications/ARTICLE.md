# Receive public applications

> Once your Career Hub is live, every published job has an apply page candidates can complete in minutes.

## Before you start
- Your Career Hub is set up and saved.
- At least one job is published — drafts and closed roles don't appear on the public page.

## Steps

1. From your Career Hub, candidates click a job card to open its details, then click **Apply**.
   ![Apply button on a job](./assets/01-apply-button.png)
2. They fill in the standard fields — name, email, phone, resume, and any custom questions you've added to that job.
3. If your job has screener questions, candidates answer them on the next step before submitting.
   ![Screener step](./assets/02-screener.png)
4. After submitting, candidates see a confirmation page. Their application appears immediately in **Applications** for the matching job, where you can review, tag, and move them through your pipeline.

## Tips
- Share the direct apply link from a job's detail page when sourcing — it skips the Career Hub home and goes straight to the form.
- Add screener questions to filter early on must-haves like work authorization or location.

## Troubleshooting
- **A job isn't showing on the public page** — confirm it's published (not draft, paused, or closed) and that its end date hasn't passed.
- **Candidates report "page not found"** — make sure your custom domain (if used) is verified, and that the job's slug hasn't changed since you shared the link.
- **Resume uploads failing** — ask the candidate to use PDF or DOCX under 10 MB.
