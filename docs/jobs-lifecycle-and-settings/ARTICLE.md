# Job lifecycle and settings

> Know the states a job moves through, and where to set the defaults that save you time on every new role.

## The lifecycle

A job moves through four main states:

1. **Draft** — you're still building it. Not visible to candidates.
2. **Published** — live and accepting applications.
3. **Paused** — temporarily hidden from candidates; the pipeline is intact.
4. **Closed** — hiring is finished. You can reopen later if needed.

![Lifecycle diagram](./assets/01-lifecycle.png)

## Where to set defaults

Open **Jobs settings** from the sidebar to manage what applies across every job:

- **Email templates** — invites, rejections, offers, and more.
- **Interview plans** — default rounds and scorecards.
- **Match-score weights** — how candidate fit is calculated.
- **Pipeline stages** — your standard pipeline shape.

![Jobs settings tabs](./assets/02-settings-tabs.png)

## Steps to change a default

1. Open **Jobs settings**.
2. Choose the tab you want to edit.
3. Make your changes and click **Save**.
   ![Save defaults](./assets/03-save.png)
4. New jobs will use the updated defaults; existing jobs keep their copy.

## Tips
- Review settings every quarter — small tweaks compound over time.
- Brief your team after big changes so they don't get caught off guard.

## Troubleshooting
- **Existing jobs didn't pick up my change** — defaults apply to new jobs only. Update existing jobs individually.
- **A setting is greyed out** — you may not have permission. Ask an admin.
