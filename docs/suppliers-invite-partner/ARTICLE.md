# Invite a partner supplier

> Send a secure onboarding link to a recruiter or agency you want to work with. They self-onboard and appear in your directory once they accept.

## Before you start
- Have the partner's company name, contact person, and email ready.
- A logo or company image is optional but helps your team recognise them in the directory.

## Steps

1. Open **Suppliers** and click **Invite supplier**. Choose the supplier type — for example, **Recruitment agency**.
   ![Open invite form](./assets/01-open-invite-form.png)
2. Fill in the company name, the contact person, and the email you want the invitation to go to. Optionally add a phone number and upload an image.
   ![Invite form](./assets/02-invite-form.png)
3. Click **Send invitation**. The supplier appears in your directory with a status of **Under review**, and the contact gets an onboarding email with a secure link valid for 7 days.

## Tips
- If you don't have an email yet, you can still create the supplier record — just leave the email blank and add it later when you're ready to invite.
- Once they accept, their status switches to **Active** automatically and they can start receiving intake requests from you.

## Troubleshooting
- **The contact says they didn't get the email** — open the supplier profile and click **Send reminder** to issue a fresh link (the previous one is invalidated).
- **The reminder button is disabled** — reminders only work while the supplier is still **Under review** and has an email on file.
