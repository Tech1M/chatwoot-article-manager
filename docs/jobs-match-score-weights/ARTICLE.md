# Configure match-score weights

> Tune the score formula so it reflects how you actually evaluate candidates.

## Before you start
- Decide which factors matter most for this role (skills vs experience vs education).
- Changes apply going forward; existing scores will recalculate within a few minutes.

## Steps

1. Open **Jobs settings** and choose the **Match-score weights** tab.
   ![Weights tab](./assets/01-weights-tab.png)
2. Adjust each slider. The total must add up to 100% — the form keeps you balanced as you drag.
   ![Sliders panel](./assets/02-sliders.png)
3. (Optional) Click **Reset to default** to start from Tech1m's recommended balance.
   ![Reset to default](./assets/03-reset.png)
4. Click **Save**. New scores roll out across all open jobs and recommendations.

## Tips
- For senior roles, weigh experience and work style higher than education.
- For early-career roles, give skills and assessments more pull.

## Troubleshooting
- **Save is disabled** — your sliders don't add up to 100%. Adjust them until the total is balanced.
- **Scores didn't change** — give it a few minutes. Re-open a candidate to refresh.
