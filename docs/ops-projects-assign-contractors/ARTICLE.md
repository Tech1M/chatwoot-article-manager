# Assign contractors to a project

> Add contractors to a project so they can log time, submit expenses, and deliver milestones — and so you can track their work in reports.

## Before you start
- The project exists and you are the owner or have edit rights.
- The contractor has an active profile in your workspace.
- You know the contractor's role, billing rate, and start date on this project.

## Steps

1. Open the project and go to the **Team** tab.
   ![Project team tab](./assets/01-team-tab.png)
2. Click **Add contractor**, search for the person, and pick them from the suggestions. You can add several contractors in one go.
   ![Add contractor dialog with search](./assets/02-add-contractor.png)
3. Set the role, billing rate, currency, and the start date for this assignment. Add an end date if it is a fixed engagement.
   ![Contractor role and rate fields](./assets/03-role-rate.png)
4. Save. The contractor is notified, the project appears in their workspace, and they can immediately log timesheets and expenses against it.

## Tips
- Re-use rate cards across projects when terms are the same — it keeps reporting consistent.
- Remove a contractor when they roll off; their past entries stay attached to the project for history.

## Troubleshooting
- **Contractor is not in the search results** — confirm they have accepted their workspace invite.
- **Rate field is locked** — only project owners or finance roles can change rates after the assignment starts.
