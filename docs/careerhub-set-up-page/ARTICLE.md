# Set up your Career Hub page

> Build a branded Career Hub that tells your story and lists every open role on a public page candidates can apply through.

## Before you start
- You're signed in as a business user with permission to manage the Career Hub.
- Your company logo, a short bio, and your social links are handy.

## Steps

1. Open **Career Hub** from the main sidebar to land on the page editor.
   ![Career Hub editor home](./assets/01-open-career-hub.png)
2. Choose a template, pick a brand color, and toggle dark mode if you'd like a darker look. The live preview updates as you edit.
3. Fill in **Company** details — name, logo, bio, mission, industry, and location. Anything you save here also powers your Career Hub header and "About" section.
   ![Company details panel](./assets/02-company-details.png)
4. Add **Culture** content: core values, benefits, testimonials, office locations, and your hiring process. Skip any sections that don't apply — empty ones won't render.
5. Under **SEO and social**, set a meta title, meta description, and a social share image so links look polished when shared.
6. Click **Save changes**, then **View page** to see your live Career Hub.
   ![Save and view page](./assets/03-save-and-view.png)

## Tips
- Your default URL is `yourcompany.careers.peoplexyz.ai` until you connect a custom domain.
- Published jobs appear automatically — you don't need to add them to the page manually.

## Troubleshooting
- **Changes not showing on the public page** — hard-refresh the live page; CDN caching can take a minute to clear.
- **Logo looks pixelated** — re-upload at 512px square or larger.
