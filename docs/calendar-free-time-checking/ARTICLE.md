# Check your free time

> Tech1m reads your connected calendars and shows you open slots so you can pick a time that actually works.

## Before you start
- At least one calendar connected under **Settings → Calendars**.
- Working hours set on your profile so suggestions land inside the times you accept meetings.

## Steps

1. Open the calendar from the sidebar, or start scheduling an interview from a candidate's profile. Use the **date range** and **duration** controls to describe the slot you want.
   ![Free time controls](./assets/01-free-time-controls.png)
2. Tech1m returns the open windows that fit. Busy events from your connected calendars are excluded automatically.
   ![Suggested free slots](./assets/02-suggested-slots.png)
3. Click a slot to use it — for example, to schedule an interview or share it with a candidate.

## Tips
- Narrow the date range to get faster, more relevant suggestions.
- If you have multiple calendars connected, all of them count as "busy". Disconnect a calendar you don't want to factor in.

## Troubleshooting
- **No slots are returned** — widen the date range, shorten the duration, or check that your working hours aren't blocking the whole window.
- **A slot you know is busy shows as free** — trigger a resync from **Settings → Calendars**, then try again.
- **Free time looks wrong by a few hours** — confirm the time zone on your profile matches the time zone in Google Calendar.
