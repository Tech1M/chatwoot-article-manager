# Manage sequence tasks

> Your daily worklist for every sourced candidate currently moving through a sequence.

## Before you start
- You need at least one active sequence with enrolled candidates.
- Have your LinkedIn or email tab open if you're sending messages manually.

## Steps

1. Go to **AI Sourcing → Sequences → Tasks**. You'll see every step that's due today or earlier, ordered by due date.
   ![Tasks queue](./assets/01-tasks-queue.png)
2. Open a task to see the candidate, the sequence, the channel, and the message template for this step.
   ![Task detail with template](./assets/02-task-detail.png)
3. Send the message in the right channel, then click **Mark as done**. Add a quick note if you want to record what happened.
4. If you can't act today, **Snooze** the task by 1–30 days, or **Skip** it to move the candidate to the next step without sending.

## Tips
- Work tasks top-to-bottom — they're already sorted by urgency.
- Use snooze sparingly; it pushes the whole sequence back for that candidate.
- Pair this with AI suggestions to draft personalized message bodies before sending.

## Troubleshooting
- **A task won't complete** — refresh the page; it may have already been actioned in another tab.
- **Tasks are missing** — only steps that are due now or earlier appear here. Steps scheduled for later will show up on their day.
