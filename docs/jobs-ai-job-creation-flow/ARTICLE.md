# Create a job with the AI flow

> Turn a short brief into a complete job — description, screener questions, and channel suggestions.

## Before you start
- Have a basic idea of the role: title, seniority, key skills, and location.
- Pick a known department; the AI uses it to tailor the draft.

## Steps

1. From the **Jobs** list, click **Create job** and choose **Use AI flow**.
   ![Choose AI flow](./assets/01-choose-ai.png)
2. Fill the short brief — title, seniority, top skills, work mode, and any extra context — then click **Continue**.
   ![AI brief form](./assets/02-brief.png)
3. Review the AI-generated draft. You'll see the description, suggested screener questions, and recommended distribution channels in a single view.
   ![Generated draft preview](./assets/03-draft.png)
4. Edit any section directly — accept what works, change what doesn't. When you're happy, continue to **Review** and publish.
   ![Edit and publish](./assets/04-publish.png)

## Tips
- The brief is short on purpose — keep it under a minute. You'll refine details on the next screen.
- After publishing, run the inclusivity and compensation analyses to catch anything the AI missed.

## Troubleshooting
- **Generation feels slow** — large drafts can take 20–30 seconds. Don't refresh; the result will appear.
- **Suggested channels seem wrong** — they're based on role and location. You can swap them on the Distribution step before publishing.
