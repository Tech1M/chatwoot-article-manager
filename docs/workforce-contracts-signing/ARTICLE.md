# Sign a contract

> Send a contractor agreement for e-signature, see who has signed, and countersign in one place.

## Before you start
- A completed contractor onboarding with the contract structure approved.
- The signing parties' names and email addresses.
- Optional: a contract template saved in **Documents**.

## Steps

1. From the contractor's profile, open the **Contracts** tab and click **Send for signature**. Tech1m pre-fills the agreement from the engagement details you set during onboarding.
   ![Send contract for signature](./assets/01-send-for-signature.png)
2. Add the signers in order — usually the contractor first, then the hiring manager or finance approver. Drag signature, date, and initial fields onto the document.
3. Add an optional message for the contractor and click **Send**. Tech1m emails each signer a secure signing link from Documenso.
   ![Drag signing fields](./assets/02-signing-fields.png)
4. Track progress on the contract row. Statuses move from **Sent** to **Viewed**, **Partially signed**, **Signed**, and finally **Completed** once everyone has signed.
5. When it is your turn, click **Sign now** to countersign without leaving Tech1m. The fully signed PDF is filed under the contractor's documents and the engagement is marked active.

## Tips
- Use a saved contract template to skip field placement on repeat engagements.
- Turn on **Reminders** to nudge signers automatically every two days.

## Troubleshooting
- **Signer says they did not get the email** — resend from the contract row and ask them to check spam. The signing link expires after seven days.
- **Status stuck on Viewed** — the signer opened the document but did not sign. Send a reminder or contact them directly.
