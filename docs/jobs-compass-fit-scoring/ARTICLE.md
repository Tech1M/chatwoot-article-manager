# Understand Compass fit scoring

> Read each candidate's Compass fit score and use it to compare like-for-like.

## Before you start
- Compass fit only appears once a candidate has applied or been added to the pipeline.
- Strong scores rely on a complete job description and clear required skills.

## Steps

1. Open the job and click an applicant. The Compass fit panel sits at the top of their profile.
   ![Compass fit panel](./assets/01-compass-panel.png)
2. The headline score combines several signals — skills match, experience, education, work style, and assessment results when available.
   ![Score breakdown](./assets/02-breakdown.png)
3. Click any component to see the underlying detail — for example, which required skills the candidate matched and which are missing.
   ![Component detail](./assets/03-component-detail.png)
4. Compare candidates side-by-side from the **Candidates** tab using the score column.

## Tips
- Treat fit as a guide, not a decision — interview signals still matter most.
- A score under 60% usually means a significant gap on a required area; check the breakdown before rejecting.

## Troubleshooting
- **Score is missing** — the candidate's profile may be incomplete, or assessment results haven't synced yet.
- **Scores look the same for everyone** — review your match weights so the components you care about have more pull.
