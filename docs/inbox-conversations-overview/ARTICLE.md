# Inbox conversations overview

> The Inbox is the single place for every message you exchange with candidates, contractors, and partners — across every job and stage.

## Before you start
- Conversations are scoped to your company. Only teammates with access to the related job or candidate see the thread.
- Each conversation is tied to a context — usually a job application, job brief, or contract — so you always know which role it relates to.

## Steps

1. Open **Inbox** from the sidebar. The left panel lists every active conversation, newest first, with the candidate or contractor's name and the latest message preview.
   ![Inbox conversation list](./assets/01-list.png)
2. Click a conversation to open it on the right. The header shows the participant, the related job or context, and who started the thread.
   ![Open conversation](./assets/02-open.png)
3. Use the search bar at the top to find a thread by candidate name or message content. Use the status filter to switch between active and archived conversations.
   ![Search and filter](./assets/03-search.png)

## Tips
- The unread count on the sidebar icon updates in real time — you don't need to refresh.
- Open the candidate's profile straight from the conversation header to check their application or notes without losing your place.

## Troubleshooting
- **Conversation isn't showing** — check the status filter; it may be archived. Switch to **Archived** to find it.
- **You can't see a thread a teammate mentioned** — you need access to the related job or candidate to see the conversation.
