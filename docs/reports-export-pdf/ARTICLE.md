# Export a report to PDF

> Turn a live report into a polished PDF you can hand to leadership or a client.

## Before you start
- Open or build the report you want to export.
- Confirm filters and date range are set the way you want them captured.

## Steps

1. Open the report from **Reports** or build a new one.
2. Click the **Export** button in the top right and choose **PDF**.
   ![Export menu](./assets/01-export-menu.png)
3. In the export dialog, pick what to include: charts, table data, filters summary, and an optional cover page with your logo and notes.
   ![PDF options](./assets/02-options.png)
4. Choose the layout — portrait for tables, landscape for wide charts.
5. Click **Generate**. The PDF appears in your downloads, and a copy is saved under the report's **Exports** tab so teammates can grab it later.
   ![Generated PDF preview](./assets/03-preview.png)

## Tips
- Add a short note on the cover page to give context — it saves a follow-up email.
- Schedule the export on a recurring basis so stakeholders always get the latest version.

## Troubleshooting
- **PDF is blank** — the report returned no rows. Adjust filters and re-run.
- **Charts look cut off** — switch to landscape layout or hide a column.
- **Logo missing** — upload one in **Settings → Branding** and try again.
