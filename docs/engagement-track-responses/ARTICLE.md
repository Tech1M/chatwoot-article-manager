# Track survey responses

> Watch responses come in, slice the data by audience, and share results with your team.

## Before you start
- You need a survey that has already been sent.
- You need view access to the Engagement Centre.

## Steps

1. Open **Engagement Centre** and click the survey you want to inspect.
   ![Survey list with response counts](./assets/01-survey-list.png)
2. The **Overview** tab shows response rate, completion rate, and average sentiment at a glance.
3. Switch to the **Responses** tab to read individual submissions. Use filters to narrow by department, location, or date.
   ![Responses tab with filters](./assets/02-responses-tab.png)
4. Open the **Insights** tab for question-level breakdowns and trend charts. Click any chart to drill into a single segment.
5. Need to share with someone else? Click **Export** to download as CSV or PDF, or use **Share link** to invite a teammate.
   ![Insights and export menu](./assets/03-insights-export.png)

## Tips
- Pin frequently used filters so they load by default next time.
- Compare cohorts (for example, hires from the last 90 days vs. tenured staff) to spot trends.

## Troubleshooting
- **Numbers haven’t updated** — refresh the page; aggregates can take a minute after a response lands.
- **Export looks empty** — check that your filter isn’t excluding all responses.
