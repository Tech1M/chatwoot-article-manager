# Submit and approve timesheets

> Contractors record their hours per period and submit for review. Your approvers sign off so payroll can run on time.

## Before you start
- The contractor is assigned to a project with a billing rate.
- Your timesheet period (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) is configured.
- A reviewer is assigned for the contractor's timesheets.

## Steps

1. Each period the contractor logs daily hours by project and submits the timesheet. You will see new entries on the **Timesheets** page with a **Pending** status.
   ![Timesheets list filtered to pending submissions](./assets/01-pending-timesheets.png)
2. Open a timesheet to view the daily breakdown, project allocation, and any notes the contractor added.
   ![Timesheet detail with daily hours breakdown](./assets/02-timesheet-detail.png)
3. Choose **Approve** to send the timesheet to payroll, or **Reject** with a reason if hours look wrong. Rejected timesheets return to the contractor for correction and resubmission.
   ![Approve and reject buttons on timesheet detail](./assets/03-approve-reject.png)
4. Approved timesheets are locked and feed into the next payroll cycle automatically.

## Tips
- Review timesheets within a day or two of submission to keep payroll on schedule.
- Use rejection reasons that are clear enough for the contractor to fix without follow-up.

## Troubleshooting
- **Hours look wrong but I cannot edit** — only the contractor can edit. Reject the timesheet so they can resubmit.
- **Timesheet missing for a contractor** — check that the contractor has an active project assignment for that period.
