# Connect your Google Calendar

> Connect Google Calendar once so Tech1m can see your busy times, place interviews on the right day, and add events to your calendar for you.

## Before you start
- A Google account you actively use for work.
- Permission to grant calendar access for that account (some Google Workspaces require admin approval).

## Steps

1. From the sidebar, open **Settings → Calendars**.
   ![Calendars settings entry](./assets/01-settings-calendars.png)
2. Click **Connect Google Calendar**. A secure window opens so you can pick the Google account you want to use and approve calendar access.
   ![Connect Google Calendar button](./assets/02-connect-google.png)
3. Once you approve, you are returned to Tech1m and your calendar shows as **Connected**. Your primary calendar is selected by default.
   ![Connected calendar status](./assets/03-connected-status.png)

## Tips
- You can connect more than one calendar. The first one you connect is treated as your default for new events.
- Free/busy data syncs in the background — give it a minute on the first connection before scheduling.

## Troubleshooting
- **The connect window closed without finishing** — try again from **Settings → Calendars**. Pop-up blockers can interrupt the sign-in window.
- **Google says your admin must approve access** — share this article with your Google Workspace admin, or pick a personal Google account instead.
- **Calendar shows connected but events aren't appearing** — see [Disconnect or resync your calendar](../calendar-disconnect-resync/ARTICLE.md).
