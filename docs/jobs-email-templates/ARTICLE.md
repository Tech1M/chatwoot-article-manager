# Manage job email templates

> Save time on candidate communication with reusable templates for every stage.

## Before you start
- Decide which stages need templates — common picks are application received, interview invite, rejection, and offer.
- Gather any standard wording or signatures your team uses.

## Steps

1. Open **Jobs settings** and switch to the **Email templates** tab.
   ![Email templates tab](./assets/01-templates-tab.png)
2. Click **New template**, give it a name, and pick the stage it applies to.
   ![New template form](./assets/02-new-template.png)
3. Write the subject and body. Use placeholders like `{{candidate_name}}` and `{{job_title}}` to personalize automatically.
   ![Editor with placeholders](./assets/03-placeholders.png)
4. Save the template. It becomes available everywhere you send that type of email.

## Tips
- Set one template per stage as the **Default** so it's pre-selected when sending.
- Preview with a real candidate before saving — placeholders are easy to mistype.

## Troubleshooting
- **Placeholder didn't render** — check spelling and that the placeholder is supported for that template type.
- **Template missing from the send dialog** — confirm the stage matches the action you're taking.
