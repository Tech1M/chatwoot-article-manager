# Approve expenses

> Look over submitted expenses, confirm the receipt and project are correct, and approve or reject so reimbursement can run.

## Before you start
- You are an approver on the relevant project or expense type.
- You know your company's expense policy (limits, allowed categories, etc.).
- You can open the receipt file types your team uses.

## Steps

1. Open **Operations → Expenses → Pending** for the queue of items waiting on you.
   ![Pending expenses queue](./assets/01-pending-expenses.png)
2. Click an expense to see the amount, project, expense type, description, and the attached receipt.
   ![Expense detail with receipt preview](./assets/02-expense-detail.png)
3. Approve, or reject with a reason. Rejected expenses return to the submitter for edits and resubmission.
   ![Approve and reject buttons on expense detail](./assets/03-approve-reject.png)
4. Approved expenses move into the next reimbursement run and appear in expense reports.

## Tips
- Spot-check that the receipt date and amount match what was entered.
- If something is borderline, ask in the comments before rejecting.

## Troubleshooting
- **Receipt will not open** — download it from the detail page and open it locally.
- **Approval did not trigger reimbursement** — reimbursement runs on a schedule; check the next run date with your finance lead.
