# Bookmark and pin posts

> Bookmarks are personal shortcuts back to a post. Pinning is the company-wide way to keep an important update visible.

## Before you start
- Anyone can bookmark any post they can see.
- Pinning is reserved for admins and post authors with the right role.

## Bookmark a post

1. Open the post menu and choose **Bookmark**. The post is added to your **Bookmarks** view.
   ![Bookmark a post](./assets/01-bookmark.png)
2. Open **Feed → Bookmarks** any time to find your saved posts. Remove a bookmark from the same menu when you no longer need it.
   ![Bookmarks view](./assets/02-bookmarks-view.png)

## Pin a post

1. Open the post menu and choose **Pin to top**. Pinned posts show at the top of the Feed for everyone in the post's audience.
   ![Pin a post](./assets/03-pin.png)
2. Unpin from the same menu when the announcement is no longer top of mind. Tech1m tracks who pinned the post for accountability.

## Tips
- Bookmark anything you'll want to reference later — onboarding info, policy updates, useful links from teammates.
- Avoid pinning more than one or two posts at a time. The more you pin, the less the pin signals "important."

## Troubleshooting
- **No Pin option** — your role doesn't include feed pinning. Ask an admin.
- **Pinned post isn't at the top** — make sure you have the **Latest** sort selected; bookmarks and pins are independent of sorting.
