# Invite guest evaluators

> Guest evaluators are external experts (for example, advisors, contractors, or partners) who join one or more interviews without becoming full staff users.

## Before you start
- Have the guest's name, email, and the role you want them to support.
- Confirm they have agreed to be invited - they will receive an email with a sign-in link.

## Steps

1. Open **Interviews -> Evaluators -> Guests** and click **Invite guest**.
   ![Invite guest dialog](./assets/01-invite-guest.png)
2. Enter the guest's email and name. Choose the jobs or interview types they may be assigned to.
3. Set an expiry date if the engagement is short term. After expiry the guest loses access automatically.
4. Send the invite. The guest receives a magic-link email; no password setup is required.
5. Once accepted, you can add the guest as an evaluator on any interview just like a staff member.
   ![Guest on interview panel](./assets/02-on-panel.png)

## Tips
- Guests only see the interviews they are assigned to and the candidate brief - never the full pipeline.
- You can revoke a guest at any time from the guest list.

## Troubleshooting
- **Guest cannot sign in** — their magic link may have expired. Resend the invite from their guest profile.
- **Guest cannot submit a scorecard** — confirm a scorecard template is attached to the interview plan.
