# Move candidates through pipeline stages

> Every job uses the same core stages — Applied, Shortlisted, Interviews, Offered, Hired — so you and your team always know where each candidate stands.

## Before you start
- Open the job and the **Applications** tab.
- Make sure the next action you'd take in real life (for example, sending an interview invite) lines up with the stage you're moving the candidate into.

## Steps

1. Open the candidate you want to move, or select several from the list.
   ![Select candidate](./assets/01-select-candidate.png)
2. Click the stage selector and pick the next stage. The candidate's card moves to that column and the team sees the change in real time.
   ![Move stage](./assets/02-move-stage.png)
3. If you select multiple candidates, choose **Move to stage** from the bulk action menu and pick a stage to update them all at once.
4. To undo a move, open the candidate and choose **Move back** — they return to their previous stage. Hired and Offered candidates can't be reverted; reach out to support if you need to.

## Tips
- Moving a candidate to **Interviews** is the right time to schedule the interview from the same page — the interview flow uses the candidate already on the application.
- If a candidate clearly isn't a fit, reject them instead of leaving them in **Applied**. Your stage counts stay accurate and reports stay clean.

## Troubleshooting
- **The next stage is greyed out** — your role may not allow that transition, or the candidate is locked in **Offered**/**Hired**.
- **A teammate's move surprised you** — open the candidate's activity log to see who moved them and when.
