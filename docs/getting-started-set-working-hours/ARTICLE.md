# Set company working hours

> Working hours are the default office hours for your workspace. Interviews, scheduling links, and reminders use them to suggest times candidates and teammates will actually show up for.

## Before you start
- Your standard work week and the time zone you operate in.
- Any company-wide breaks (lunch, daily standup) you want to block out.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings → Company → Working hours**.
   ![Working hours settings](./assets/01-working-hours-settings.png)
2. Pick your default **time zone**. Each teammate can override their personal time zone later.
   ![Time zone picker](./assets/02-timezone.png)
3. For each day of the week, toggle it on and set start and end times. Add a midday break if you need one.
   ![Per-day hours](./assets/03-day-hours.png)
4. Click **Save**. Scheduling tools across the workspace start using these hours immediately.

## Tips
- Teammates can set their own working hours from their profile — those override the company defaults for their own calendar.
- Working hours are a guide, not a hard wall. You can still book meetings outside them when you need to.

## Troubleshooting
- **Interviews are being suggested at odd hours** — check that the candidate's time zone matches what you expect, and that the interviewer's personal hours aren't overriding the company default.
- **Save button is disabled** — only Owners and Admins can change company working hours.
