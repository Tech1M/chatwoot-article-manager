# Generate a hero image

> Skip the design back-and-forth. Describe the vibe you want and let Tech1m generate a hero image tailored to your Career Hub.

## Before you start
- Your company name, industry, and bio are filled out in Career Hub — we use them to ground the image.
- Have a one- or two-sentence description of the mood you want (e.g. "warm, collaborative team in a sunlit office").

## Steps

1. Open **Career Hub** and find the **Hero image** card in the layout panel.
   ![Hero image card](./assets/01-hero-image-card.png)
2. Click **Generate with AI**. Type a short prompt describing the look — colors, mood, and any visual cues you want.
   ![Generate hero prompt](./assets/02-generate-prompt.png)
3. Click **Generate**. The new image replaces any previous AI-generated hero and appears in your live preview.
4. Save your changes to publish the new hero on your public Career Hub.

## Tips
- Keep prompts concrete: "diverse engineering team at whiteboard, soft daylight" beats "modern tech office".
- Don't love the result? Run it again — each generation is fresh, and you can keep trying until it fits.
- You can also upload your own hero image instead of generating one.

## Troubleshooting
- **"Unable to generate image right now"** — try again in a moment; this usually means the AI service was briefly busy.
- **The image looks off-brand** — add your brand color or industry to the prompt for closer results.
