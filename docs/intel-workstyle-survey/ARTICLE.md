# Run a workstyle survey

> Find out how your team likes to work — communication, pace, decision-making — and use it to improve collaboration.

## Before you start
- Confirm the team or department you want to survey has accounts in Tech1m.
- Decide whether responses should be anonymous; you cannot change this after launch.

## Steps

1. Go to **Workforce intelligence** and click the **Workstyle** tab, then **New survey**.
   ![Start a workstyle survey](./assets/01-start.png)
2. Pick a **template** — full workstyle, communication-only, or decision-making-only — or start from scratch.
3. Choose your **audience**. You can select a team, a department, or specific people.
   ![Audience picker](./assets/02-audience.png)
4. Set the **window** for responses (typically 5 to 10 days) and toggle anonymity if needed.
5. Review the invite message, then click **Launch**. Tech1m sends invites and reminders automatically.
6. Once results come in, open the **Results** tab to see team-level patterns and individual workstyle profiles.
   ![Results view](./assets/03-results.png)

## Tips
- Share the team-level summary in your next all-hands; it sparks better conversations than a slide of scores.
- Re-run the survey every 6 months to see how the team evolves.

## Troubleshooting
- **Low response rate** — extend the window and send a manual nudge from the survey page.
- **Cannot see individual profiles** — the survey was launched anonymously; only aggregate views are available.
