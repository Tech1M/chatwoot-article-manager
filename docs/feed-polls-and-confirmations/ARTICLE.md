# Create polls and confirmations

> Polls give you fast group input. Confirmations give you a paper trail that everyone has read a critical update.

## Before you start
- Both options live inside the **New post** flow.
- Confirmations are best for policy changes, compliance updates, and anything you'd otherwise email and ask people to acknowledge.

## Create a poll

1. Open **New post**, switch to the **Poll** tab, and add your question. Add up to several answer options.
   ![Poll setup](./assets/01-poll.png)
2. Choose whether responses are visible to everyone or anonymous, then post. Results update live as people vote.
   ![Poll results](./assets/02-results.png)

## Require a confirmation

1. In **New post**, toggle on **Require confirmation**. Recipients see a **Mark as read** button on the post.
   ![Confirmation toggle](./assets/03-confirmation.png)
2. Track responses from the post's actions menu — you'll see who has confirmed and who still needs to.

## Tips
- Keep polls to one clear question with three to five options. More than that and engagement drops sharply.
- For confirmations, write the action you want in the post body, e.g., "Read the new expense policy."

## Troubleshooting
- **People can't vote** — check the post's visibility; only the audience you selected can interact.
- **Confirmation count looks wrong** — refresh; counts update in real time but may lag during heavy activity.
