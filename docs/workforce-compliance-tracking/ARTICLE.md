# Track compliance

> See every contractor's compliance status, spot gaps before they become risks, and re-run checks when something expires.

## Before you start
- At least one onboarded contractor.
- The compliance regimes that apply to your business (IR35, CIS, AWR, RTW, background checks).

## Steps

1. Open **Contractors** and switch to the **Compliance** view. Each row shows one contractor with badges for IR35, CIS, AWR, RTW, and background checks.
   ![Compliance overview](./assets/01-compliance-overview.png)
2. Filter by status — **At risk**, **Expiring soon**, or **Expired** — to focus on contractors who need attention this week.
3. Open a contractor to see the full compliance timeline: each determination, the questions answered, the supporting documents, and who recorded them.
   ![Contractor compliance timeline](./assets/02-compliance-timeline.png)
4. Click **Re-run check** to refresh a determination when circumstances change — for example a new statement of work, an extension, or a renewed visa. The new outcome is added to the timeline; the previous one is archived, not lost.
5. Use **Export** to download a CSV of the current compliance posture for audit, internal review, or sharing with a client.

## Tips
- Pin the **Expiring in 30 days** filter to your dashboard so renewals never sneak up on you.
- Compliance reminders go to the contractor and the point of contact automatically — you do not need to chase manually.

## Troubleshooting
- **A badge is grey instead of green or red** — the regime does not apply to that engagement (for example, CIS only applies to UK construction roles).
- **Re-run check is disabled** — the contract is paused or ended. Reactivate the engagement first.
