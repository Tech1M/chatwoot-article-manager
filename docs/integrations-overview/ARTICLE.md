# Integrations overview

> Connect Tech1m to your existing ATS, calendar, and productivity tools so your team can hire without juggling tabs.

## Before you start
- You'll need the **Manage integrations** permission. Owners and admins have it by default.
- You'll need a working login for the tool you want to connect (for example, your Greenhouse or Workable account).

## What integrations do

Integrations let Tech1m read from and write to the tools your company already runs on. Once connected, you can:

1. Pull candidates and pipelines from your existing ATS into Tech1m talent pools.
   ![Active integrations on the Integrations page](./assets/01-active-integrations.png)
2. Sync calendars so interviewers' availability is always live.
3. Send messages, files, and updates to the channels your team watches.
   ![Provider categories on the marketplace](./assets/02-categories.png)

Each connection is private to the teammate who set it up. If you connect your ATS, your colleague still needs to connect theirs to import using their own account.

## Where to find integrations
- **Settings → Integrations** lists everything available, with the ones you've already connected pinned at the top.
- Categories like **ATS**, **Calendar**, **Communication**, and **Storage** help narrow the marketplace.

## Tips
- Start with the ATS your recruiters use most — it unlocks candidate import right away.
- Keep one teammate's connection per provider unless you genuinely need separate accounts. Duplicates can confuse imports.

## Troubleshooting
- **You don't see the Integrations menu** — your role may not include integrations access. Ask an admin to grant the permission.
- **A provider isn't listed** — see [Request a new integration](../integrations-request-new/ARTICLE.md).
