# Post a company update

> The Feed is your internal social space — use it for announcements, kudos, and updates that the whole company (or a specific group) should see.

## Before you start
- Anyone with Feed access can post. Pinning and company-wide visibility may require an admin role.
- Targeted posts only show to the people or departments you select — they don't spam the rest of the company.

## Steps

1. Open the **Feed** from the sidebar and click **New post**. Type your update — you can add images, links, and rich text.
   ![New feed post](./assets/01-new-post.png)
2. Choose a visibility option. **Company** sends it to everyone with Feed access. **Targeted** lets you pick specific departments or teammates.
   ![Visibility options](./assets/02-visibility.png)
3. Click **Post**. Your update appears at the top of the Feed for everyone in scope, and recipients get a notification.
   ![Published post](./assets/03-published.png)

## Tips
- Toggle off comments or reactions on a post when you want a one-way announcement.
- Lead with a clear headline — Feed posts are skimmed, not read in full.

## Troubleshooting
- **Post isn't showing for a teammate** — check visibility; targeted posts only reach selected people or departments.
- **Image won't upload** — Tech1m supports common image formats up to a few MB. Try a smaller file.
