# Extend a job posting

> Keep a job open by pushing the deadline forward — no need to repost.

## Before you start
- Confirm hiring is still active for this role.
- Check whether your distribution channels have their own expiry rules.

## Steps

1. Open the job and click **More actions** in the top-right.
   ![More actions menu](./assets/01-more-actions.png)
2. Choose **Extend posting**.
   ![Extend option](./assets/02-extend-option.png)
3. Pick a new closing date and add an optional note for your team. Click **Save**.
   ![Pick new date](./assets/03-pick-date.png)
4. The job's expiry updates everywhere it's distributed.

## Tips
- Extend before the original date passes — expired jobs are removed from external channels and may need a new push to come back.
- Use a short note like "Extended to widen senior candidate pool" so teammates understand the change later.

## Troubleshooting
- **Extend option is missing** — only published jobs can be extended. Reopen a closed job first.
- **External board still shows the old date** — some channels refresh on a daily schedule. Allow up to 24 hours.
