# Use curated candidate recommendations

> Get a shortlist of likely-fit candidates without running a separate search.

## Before you start
- Make sure the job description and required skills are filled in — recommendations rely on them.
- A populated talent pool gives you stronger matches.

## Steps

1. Open the job and click the **Curated** tab.
   ![Curated tab](./assets/01-curated-tab.png)
2. Scan the recommended candidates. Each one shows a fit score and the top reasons they matched.
   ![Recommended candidates list](./assets/02-recommended.png)
3. Click a candidate to view their full profile, then choose **Add to pipeline** to start them as an applicant.
   ![Add to pipeline](./assets/03-add-to-pipeline.png)
4. Use the filters at the top to narrow by experience, location, or skills.

## Tips
- Add the candidate to a talent pool first if you're not ready to invite them — you can revisit later.
- Refine your job's required skills if recommendations feel off — better inputs make better matches.

## Troubleshooting
- **No recommendations are showing** — the job may be brand-new. Wait a few minutes and refresh.
- **The same candidates keep appearing** — they're already strong matches. Adjust skill weights to surface different profiles.
