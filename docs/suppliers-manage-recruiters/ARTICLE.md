# Manage recruiter suppliers

> Once a recruiter has onboarded, you can assign them to roles, brief them with an intake request, and watch their progress from a single profile.

## Before you start
- The recruitment agency must be **Active** in your supplier directory. If they're still under review, see [Invite a partner supplier](../suppliers-invite-partner/ARTICLE.md).
- Have the role or job brief you want them to work on ready to share.

## Steps

1. Open **Suppliers** and click into the recruitment agency you want to manage. The profile shows their contact details, status, and any intake requests already shared with them.
   ![Recruiter profile](./assets/01-recruiter-profile.png)
2. To brief them on a new role, click **Send intake request** and pick the job brief. Set the number of openings, priority, and the hiring manager so the agency knows exactly what's needed.
   ![Send intake](./assets/02-send-intake.png)
3. Track their work in the **Intake requests** panel on the profile. Each request shows the status, the linked job brief, and the hiring manager you assigned.

## Tips
- Use priority and openings consistently across agencies — that's how their dashboard ranks the work they show you.
- Keep the hiring manager field accurate; when an agency has a question, this is who they'll route it to.

## Troubleshooting
- **The agency says they can't see the brief** — check the intake request status; it may be in draft. Send it from the profile to publish it to them.
- **A recruiter has gone quiet** — open their profile, look at the latest intake request, and use the messaging or email contact to follow up.
