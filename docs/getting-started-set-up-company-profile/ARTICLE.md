# Set up your company profile

> Your company profile is what candidates and teammates see across job posts, emails, and your career page. Set it up once and it shows up everywhere.

## Before you start
- A square logo (PNG or SVG, at least 256×256).
- Your company website, registered address, and main contact email.

## Steps

1. From the sidebar, open **Settings → Company**.
   ![Company settings entry](./assets/01-settings-company.png)
2. Upload your **logo** and, optionally, a banner image. Pick the brand color used on your career page and outbound emails.
   ![Logo and branding fields](./assets/02-logo-branding.png)
3. Fill in your legal name, website, country, time zone, and primary address. The time zone is used as the default for job posts and interview scheduling.
   ![Company details form](./assets/03-company-details.png)
4. Click **Save**. Changes apply across your workspace immediately.

## Tips
- Keep your logo on a transparent background — it will sit on both light and dark surfaces.
- The display name is what candidates see; you can keep your legal entity separate if they differ.

## Troubleshooting
- **Logo looks blurry on the career page** — re-upload at a larger size; very small images get scaled up.
- **Can't save changes** — only workspace owners and admins can edit the company profile. Ask an owner if your buttons are greyed out.
